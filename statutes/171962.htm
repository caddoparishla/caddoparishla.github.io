Title 17. Education

Chapter 8. Education of Students with Exceptionalities

Part II. Jimmy D. Long, SR. Louisiana School for Math, Science, and the Arts

 

17:1962      Definitions

 

As used in this Part, the following words, terms and phrases shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this Section, except when the context clearly indicates a different meaning: 

 

(1) “Department” means the state department of education. 

 

(2) “School” means the Jimmy D. Long, Sr. Louisiana School for Math, Science, and the Arts. 

 

(3) “State Board” means the State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education. 

 

(4) “State superintendent” means the state superintendent of education. 

 

Added by Acts 1981, No. 932, § 1, eff. Sept. 1, 1982. Amended by Acts 1982, No. 151, § 1, eff. July 12, 1982; Acts 2017, No. 374, § 2, eff. June 23, 2017.
