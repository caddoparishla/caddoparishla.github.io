TITLE 39. PUBLIC FINANCE

SUBTITLE II. LOCAL FINANCE

CHAPTER 7. LOCAL DEPOSITORIES

PART I. GENERAL PROVISIONS

 

39:1233.1      Bank officer, director, or employee who is member of depositing authority; recusal

 Notwithstanding any provision of Chapter 15 of Title 42 of the Revised Statutes of 1950 [FN1] or any other law to the contrary, any member of a local depositing authority, including the chief executive officer thereof, may serve as an officer, director, or employee, whether compensated or not, of any national or state bank; provided that he shall recuse himself from voting in favor of any such bank and shall disclose the reason for such recusal by filing same into the minutes or record of the local depositing authority and by forwarding a disclosure form to the Board of Ethics. The Board of Ethics shall develop, in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act, [FN2] a disclosure form to be utilized in complying with the provisions of this Section.
