TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 15. EDUCATIONAL COOPERATIVE LAW

 

17:2805      Name of cooperative

  The name of each cooperative shall include the words "Educational" and "Cooperative," and the abbreviation "Inc.,". The name of a cooperative shall distinguish it from the name of any other corporation organized under the laws of, or authorized to transact business in, this state. The words "Educational" and "Cooperative" shall not both be used in the name of any corporation organized under the laws of, or authorized to transact business in, this state, except a cooperative or a corporation transacting business in this state pursuant to the provisions of this Chapter.

Added by Acts 1958, No. 257, Sec. 5.
