January 10, 2022

CPSB Special Session

 

The Caddo Parish School Board (CPSB) met in Special Session on January 10, 2023, at 4:30 p.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, Louisiana.  Board members present were Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Barbara Smith-Iverson, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell.   Also present were Superintendent Lamar Goree and Legal Counsel Reginald Abrams.  

 

Dottie Bell, 1st Vice President, called the meeting to order at approximately 4:30 p.m. Steve Umling led the Invocation and the Pledge of Allegiance.  

 

Charge to Caddo Parish School Board Members Former Caddo Parish School Board President Denee’ Locke served on the Board from 2015-2020 as the District 8 representative.  During that time, she was named President in 2017 and was chairwoman of numerous Board committees.  She also supported initiatives that championed classroom teachers and students and sought fiscal responsibility.  Additionally, she promoted board member ethics and order and worked closely with board members to commission the creation of the board’s Code of Ethics.  Mrs. Locke delivered a Charge to the Board as members prepared to be sworn in. Dr. Goree echoed comments by Mrs. Locke and thanked each Board member for their willingness to serve on the School Board.  He said he is encouraged that great things will be accomplished over the next four years, and he looks forward to working with the Board.

 

Administer Oath of Office for CPSB Members Mr. Reginald Abrams administered the oath of office for the Caddo Parish School Board Members.

 

Visitors

None

 

Election of 2023 CPSB Officers Mr. Rachal moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, that the officers be elected by slate. The following slate was nominated: President – Dr. Terence Vinson; 1st Vice President – Don Little; 2nd Vice President; – Mary Trammel. A substitute motion was made by Dr. Douzart, seconded by Ms. Green, to nominate the following slate of officers: President - Dr. Terence Vinson; 1st Vice President – Don Little; 2nd Vice President – Jasmine Green.  Vote on the motion carried, with Mary Trammel, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell opposed.

 

Authorize Use of Facsimile Signature by President Mr. Rachal moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to authorize the President to use a facsimile signature.  Vote on the motion carried unanimously.

 

Mrs. Bell announced that following the adjournment of this meeting, there would be a 15-30 minute recess before the Executive Committee Work Session begins.

 

Adjournment Mr. Rachal moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to adjourn.  The vote on the motion to adjourn carried, and the meeting adjourned at approximately 5:00 p.m.
