TITLE 49. STATE ADMINISTRATION
CHAPTER 1. GENERAL PROVISIONS

PART VIII. STATE SYMBOLS AND DISPLAY OF FLAGS

 

49:152       United States flag to be displayed

 The flag of the United States shall wave from sunrise to sunset every day, over the State Capitol and the public departments and institutions of the state and over the court houses in the several parishes during the sessions of the courts. Public officers shall enforce and execute the provisions of this Section.
