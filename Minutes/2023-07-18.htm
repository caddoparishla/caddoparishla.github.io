July 18, 2023

CPSB Meeting

 

Members present Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell. Also present were Dr. T. Lamar Goree, Secretary, and Mr. Reggie Abrams, Board Counsel.

13.02 Request for Settlement Authority – Claimant: James Curry – Date of Loss 11/11/19 – Claim #7020795. The Board approved the settlement as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.02 Consideration of enacting Policy for the nominating procedure for Electing School Board Officers Mr. Little moved, seconded by Ms. Green, to enact Policy for the nominating procedure for electing School Board Officers as recommended by Board Member Don Little and submitted in the electronic mailout. After a lengthy discussion, Mrs. Yeates called for the question, seconded by Mrs. Bell, to end the debate.  The vote on the motion carried, and the discussion ended with Mrs. Tharpe and Mrs. McLain opposed. Mrs. Bell moved, seconded by Mr. Rachal, to refer this agenda item to the Policy Committee. The vote on the motion failed, with Mr. Umling, Ms. Green, Mr. Little, Dr. Douzart, Mrs. Tharpe, Mrs. McLain, and Mrs.Yeates opposed. Further discussion ensued, and Ms. Trammel moved, seconded by Ms. Green, to call for the question to end the debate. The vote on the motion carried, and the discussion ended with Mrs. McLain opposed. The vote on the original motion carried to enact a policy for the nominating procedure for electing School Board Officers, with Mrs. Bell opposed.

 

8.05 Consideration to approve the renaming of Northside Elementary School to Linear Middle School After a lengthy discussion, Mr. Rachal moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to call for the question to end the debate.  The vote on the motion carried, with Ms. Green, Dr. Douzart, Mrs. McLain, Mrs. Yeates, and Mrs. Bell opposed. Mr. Rachal moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to postpone the vote, based on the recommendation of the Board Counsel, on renaming Northside Elementary School to Linear Middle School. The vote on the motion carried, with Ms. Green, Dr. Vinson, Dr. Douzart, and Mrs. Bell opposed.

 

ANNOUNCEMENTS Dr. Goree reminded the Board of “Ring the Bell” at 9 am on August 9th at Green Oaks High School.  He also invited the board to Summer Graduation at Huntington High School on August 10th at 6 pm.  Mr. Little encouraged the board that if they had not seen the movie “Sound of Freedom,” they should strongly consider doing so.  Dr. Douzart thanked the Board for the gift in memory of the passing of her brother. Mrs. Bell reminded the board of her special event on Thursday, July 20th from 1 pm – 3 pm.

 

ADJOURNMENT Mrs. Yeates moved, seconded by Mrs. Bell, to adjourn.  The vote on the motion carried, and the July 18, 2023, CPSB Meeting adjourned at approximately 7:50 p.m.
