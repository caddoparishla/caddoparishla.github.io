PROHIBITION OF UTILIZING LEWD, PORNOGRAPHIC,

SEXUALLY EXPLICIT OR VULGAR CONTENT

 

 

The Caddo Parish School Board strictly prohibits the creation, hosting, or promotion of events or materials that are lewd, pornographic, sexually explicit or vulgar in nature to students.

 

Exemptions to include scientifically accurate information about human and non-human development, biology, anatomy and reproductive health when the content is age and contextually appropriate.

 

New policy:  September 19, 2023
