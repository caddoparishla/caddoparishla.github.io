8.06 Election of Officers

 

Mr. Reginald Abrams, Board Counsel, announced the names of the board members submitted by Nominating Chair Katie McLain, who showed interest in being on the ballot as an officer on the Caddo Parish School Board Executive Committee.  Mr. Don Little

requested his name be removed from the list of candidates for Caddo Parish School Board Second Vice President.

 

President

 

Mr. Abrams announced the board would vote for President, and he asked (three times) if anyone wanted to add their name to the list of candidates.  There were no additional candidates; therefore, the voting process began for Board President:

 

Dr. Bonita Douzart (1 yes vote by Dr. Douzart)

Jasmine Green (9 yes votes – Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Don Little, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Jerry Bowman, Jr., Barry Rachal, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell)

Jasmine Green was named 2025 President of the Caddo Parish School Board.

 

First Vice President

 

Mr. Abrams announced the board would vote on the First Vice President, and he asked (three times) if anyone wanted to add their name to the list of candidates.  Dr. Vinson nominated Steve Umling as the First Vice President, and Mr. Umling accepted the

nomination; therefore, the voting process began for the Board's First Vice President.

 

Steve Umling    (4 yes votes - Steve Umling, Dr. Terence Vinson, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel)

Jessica Yeates (7 yes votes – Don Little, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Jerry Bowman, Jr., Barry Rachal, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell)

Jessica Yeates was named 2025 First Vice President of the Caddo Parish School Board.

 

Board Member Don Little called a point of order/clarification and inquired if Board Member Trammel voted "yes" for both candidates, Steve Umling and Jessica Yeates.  Ms. Trammel's microphone was not turned on by President Yeates; therefore, her response

was not recorded.  Superintendent Burton stated he heard Ms. Trammel say she intended to vote for Mrs. Yeates.  

 

Second Vice President

 

Mr. Abrams announced the board would vote for Second Vice President, and he asked (three times) if anyone wanted to add their name to the list of candidates.  There were no additional candidates; therefore, the voting began for Second Vice President.  Board

Member Trammel was the only candidate, and she won, being she was unopposed.

 

Mary Trammel was named 2025 Second Vice President of the Caddo Parish School Board.

12.01 Board Member Announcements and Requests

 

Board President Yeates gave her farewell speech.  Dottie Bell thanked the board for accepting her as First Vice President on the CPSB Executive Committee and stated that she and President Yeates worked well together in 2024.  Mr. Rachal gave his two board

requests and asked for a response.  He also announced that the CPSB Board Christmas Luncheon was incredible.  He praised President Yeates for serving during a tough time during the Superintendent Search.  Ms. Trammel congratulated President Green on

being selected as the 2025 CPSB President.  Jasmine Green said she looked forward to serving and said, "Get Ready." Dr. Douzart thanked the new Superintendent and our new Board President and said she hoped they would continue to work together.  Dr.

Douzart also thanked Mr. Greer for the paved parking lot surrounding the Professional Development Center.  Mr. Bowman thanked Mr. Burton for the lunch, and the dialogue helped him get to know each person on the board.  He thanked the board for appointing

him to service District 7, and he looks forward to the spring election.  Mr. Umling thanked Mrs. Yeates for serving as Board President and Mr. Burton for today's meal.  He wished everyone a Merry Christmas.  Mr. Burton thanked President Yeates and looks

forward to working with President Green, 1st VP Yeates, and 2nd VP Trammel.  Mrs. Bell thanked everyone who supported her Party with a Purpose.  She congratulated Ms. Green and said she had some big shoes to fill.  And Ms. Bell wished everyone a Merry

Christmas.
