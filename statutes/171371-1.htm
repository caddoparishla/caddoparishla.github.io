TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 3. SCHOOL DISTRICTS

 

17:1371.1    School districts formed under R.S. 17:1371; confirmation

  School districts heretofore formed in a manner permitted by R.S. 17:1371 as amended by Act 159 of 1950, are hereby ratified and confirmed, to the same extent as though said authority existed at the time of their formation or establishment.

Added by Acts 1950, No. 159, Sec. 2.
