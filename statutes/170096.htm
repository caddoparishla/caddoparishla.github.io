TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 1. GENERAL SCHOOL LAW
PART II. PARISH SCHOOL BOARD

SUBPART B. POWERS AND DUTIES OF SCHOOL BOARDS AND PARISH SUPERINTENDENTS

 

17:96       Treasurer's duty to demand funds from predecessor

  The treasurer, immediately upon his appointment, shall demand of his predecessor in the office of treasurer, the school funds in his hands as custodian of the school funds of the parish.
