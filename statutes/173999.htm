Title 17. Education

Chapter 42. Charter School Demonstration Programs Law

Part V. Operation of a Charter School

 

17:3999         Application of Chapter

 

A. All charter schools shall be governed by the law in effect on August 15, 2003. Thereafter, if the provisions of this Chapter are amended, all charter schools shall comply with the law as amended within ninety days of its effective date. 

 

B. To the extent that the provisions of this Chapter conflict with the provisions of R.S. 17:10.7.1, the provisions of R.S. 17:10.7.1 shall prevail.
