TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 1. GENERAL SCHOOL LAW
PART II. PARISH SCHOOL BOARDS

SUBPART B. POWERS AND DUTIES OF SCHOOL BOARDS AND PARISH SUPERINTENDENTS

 

17:99       Investment of funds

 Parish and city school boards may invest any funds which they may have on hand, from whatever source derived, in direct obligations of the United States government and in time certificates of deposit of state banks organized under the laws of Louisiana and national banks having their principal office in the state of Louisiana.

Amended by Acts 1966, No. 162, Sec. 1; Acts 1995, No. 1126, Sec. 2, eff. June 29, 1995.
