November 28, 2023

CPSB Meeting

 

Members presentSteve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little,  Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Christine Tharpe, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell. Also present were Dr. T. Lamar Goree, Secretary, and Mr. Reggie Abrams, Board Counsel.

 

President Dr. Terence Vinson called the November 28, 2023, CPSB meeting to order, and Board Member  Dottie Bell gave the Invocation and led the Pledge of Allegiance.

 

CONSIDERATION OF THE MINUTES OF THE OCTOBER 3, 2023, AND OCTOBER 17, 2023, CPSB MEETINGS. Darrin Dixon moved, seconded by Mary Trammel, to approve the minutes of the October 3, 2023, and October 17, 2023, CPSB meetings as submitted in the electronic mailout.  Vote on the motion carried unanimously.

 

PRESENTATIONS AND RECOGNITIONS. None

 

VISITORS. The following visitors addressed the Board on matters of interest/concern:  Richard Dugans, Shreveport, LA, would like to utilize the vacant buildings owned by CPSB to house people experiencing homelessness; Tamika Freelieu Hamilton, Shreveport, LA, thanked the Board for the supplement just received and asked the board to seek other insurance companies before raising the premiums;  Jon Glover, Shreveport, LA concerned with the school performance scores, especially on the Elementary level and suggested the district needs to do something different than what has been done;  Jordan Thomas, Red River United, voiced her concern about the changes in the health insurance.

 

ESTABLISH THE AGENDA AND PROPOSED CONSENT AGENDA. Superintendent Goree highlighted items for the Board’s consideration.  President Vinson announced the following items are on consent: 6.01-6.04, 7.01, 8.01-8.03, 8.05-8.07, 8.11, and 13.01.

 

Jessica Yates moved, seconded by Katie McLain,  to establish the agenda and consent agenda for the November 28, 2023 CPSB meeting as presented. Vote on the motion carried.  

 

CONFIRM THE CONSENT AGENDA. Jasmine Green moved, seconded by Jessica Yeates, to confirm the consent agenda for the November 28, 2023, CPSB Meeting.  Vote on the motion carried, and the following is a summary of the Board’s action on the consent agenda items.

 

6.01 Personnel Recommendations. The Board approved Personnel Recommendations as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

6.02 Personnel Leaves.  The Board approved the Personnel Leaves as recommended by the Superintendent and submitted in the electronic mailout

 

6.03 Personnel Resignations.  The Board approved the Personnel Resignations as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

6.04 Administrative Contract Renewals.  The Board approved the Administrative Contract Renewals as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

7.01 Capital Project Bids.The Board approved the bid as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout on the bid tabulation sheets: 1) Project 2025-800 “Fair Park HVAC Upgrade, Phase 2”, be awarded to Fitzgerald Contractors (Not OCC*), Shreveport, LA, with a Base Bid, Alternate 1, Alternate 2, and Alternate 3 for the sum total of $1,906,800.00. The budget amount is $2,000,000.00. Charge to ESSER Funds 2) Project 2025-804 “Caddo Magnet High HVAC,” be awarded to The Payne Company (Not OCC*), Shreveport, LA, with a Base Bid, Alternate 1, Alternate 2 for the sum total of $1,339,000.00. The budget amount is $1,000,000.00. Charge to ESSER Funds. 3) Project 2025-805 “Caddo Middle Magnet HVAC,” be awarded to Bobby Brannon Heating & Air (Not OCC*), Shreveport, LA, with a Base Bid, Alternate 1, and Alternate 2 for the sum total of $665,608.00. The budget amount is $600,000.00. Charge to ESSER Funds.

 

8.01 Property/Bus Rental Requests The Board approved property/bus rental requests as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.02 To consider and take action with respect to adopting a resolution authorizing the incurring of debt and issuance of General Obligation School Bonds, Series 2024, of the Parishwide School District of Caddo Parish, Louisiana, and providing for other matters in connection herewith as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout The Board approved to adopt a resolution authorizing the incurring of debt and issuance of General Obligation School Bonds, Series 2024, of the Parishwide School District of Caddo Parish, Louisiana; and providing for other matters in connection herewith as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.03 Consideration to approve the C.E. Byrd/LSUS Baseball field proposal. The Board approved the C.E. Byrd/LSUS Baseball field proposal for lease and funding of the access road as presented to the Building and Grounds Committee and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.05 Consideration to approve revisions to CPSB Policy BCAD - Teleconference/Remote Participation in School Board Meetings.  The Board approved revision to CPSB Policy BCAD-Teleconference/Remote Participation in School Board Meetings as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.06 Consideration to approve revision to CPSB Policy BCBI-Public Participation in School Board Meetings.  The Board approved revision to CPSB Policy BCBI-Public Participation in School Board Meetings as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout. 

 

8.07 Consideration of Resolution in Recognition of Speaker of the House of Representatives, Mike Johnson. The Board approved the Resolution in Recognition of Speaker of the House of Representatives, Mike Johnson, as recommended by Board Member Don Little and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.11 Consideration to approve the property located at 1950 Northside Road be listed for sale through our listing agent at UL Coleman, Inc.  The Board approved the property located at 1950 Northside Road to be listed for sale through our listing agent at U.L. Coleman, Inc. as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

13.01 Student Readmission Appeal Hearings.  The Board approved staff recommendations for students KB and KC as parents are in agreement as submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.04 Consideration to begin the process to change the name of Northside Middle School to Linear Middle School. Ms. Green moved, seconded by Mrs. Bell, to begin the process to change the name of Northside Middle School to Linear Northside Middle School. After lengthy discussion, Mr. Dixon moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to call for the question to end debate. The vote on the motion carried, with Mrs. Tharpe, Mrs. McLain, Mrs. Yeates, and Mrs. Bell opposed. A substitute motion was made by Mr. Little, seconded by Mrs. Tharpe, to postpone the vote to begin the process to change the name of Northside Middle School to Linear Middle School.  The vote on the motion carried, with Ms. Green, Ms. Trammel, Mr. Dixon, Mrs. Yeates, and Mrs. Bell opposed.

 

8.08 Consideration to approve transfers to the Athletic Fields Reserve and Financial/HR Services System Upgrade Reserve. Dr. Douzart moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to approve transfers to the Athletic Fields Reserve and Financial/HR Services System Upgrade Reserve. Mrs. McLain moved, seconded by Mrs. Yeates, to remove the $3M transfer to the Athletic Fields Reserve for the turf field at North Caddo High School. The vote on the motion failed, with Ms. Green, Dr. Vinson, Mr. Little, Dr. Douzart, Ms. Trammel, Mr. Dixon, and Mrs. Bell opposed.  The vote on the original motion made by Dr. Douzart, seconded by Ms. Trammel to approve transfers to the Athletic Fields Reserve and Financial/HR Services System Upgrade Reserve carried, with Mrs. McLain opposed.

 

8.09 Consideration to approve staff’s recommendation to proceed with implementation of the Blue Advantage Plan. Mrs. Bell moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to approve staff’s recommendation to proceed with the implementation of the optional Blue Advantage Plan as submitted in the electronic mailout.  The vote on the motion carried unanimously.

 

8.10 Consideration to authorize staff to work through our listing agent at U.L. Coleman, Inc. to negotiate terms for the purchase of property located at 2020 Midway Avenue.  Dr. Douzart moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to authorize staff to work through our Listing Agent from U.L. Coleman, Inc. to negotiate terms for the purchase of the property located at 2020 Midway Avenue as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout. The vote on the motion carried unanimously.

 

ANNOUNCEMENTS. Mr. Umling thanked the staff for their hard work that contributed to increased school performance scores. Ms. Trammel congratulated Mrs. Bell on the Bayou Classic win. Ms. Green stated she wants the renaming of Northside to Linear brought back to the board as soon as possible. Mrs. Bell congratulated all schools for their increased performance, which was reflected in the school performance scores.  She explained the 8th annual Party with a Purpose was a great success and thanked those on the board who joined her in serving. Mr. Dixon stated he was very proud of the district's gains and excited to be moving forward.  He also invited the board to a Toy Drive he has participated in since 2009.  It will be held at the LA Daquiri Café on December 14th at 5:30 p.m. Mr. Little wished everyone Happy Holidays.

 

ADJOURNMENT. Mrs. Yeates moved, seconded by Ms. Green, to adjourn.  The vote on the motion carried, and the November 28, 2023, CPSB Meeting adjourned at approximately

7:15 p.m.
