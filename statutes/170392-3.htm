TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 1. GENERAL SCHOOL LAW

PART VIA. SCREENING AND INTERVENTION FOR SCHOOL SUCCESS

 

17:392.3         Implementation

 It is the intention of the legislature that the costs relative to the implementation of the provisions of this Part shall be covered by funds appropriated by the state. Such funds shall include those appropriated pursuant to the Minimum Foundation Program.
