Title 17. Education

Chapter 1. General School Law

Part III. Public Schools and School Children

Subpart B-1. Louisiana Environmental Education Act

 

 

17:203      Definitions

 

As used in this Subpart, the following terms have the following meanings unless the context requires otherwise: 

 

(1) “Commission” means the Louisiana Environmental Education Commission. 

 

(2) “Department” means the state Department of Education. 

 

(3) to (6) Repealed by Acts 2022, No. 16, § 3. 

 

(7) “Section” means the environmental education section located within and acting through the state Department of Education. 

 

Redesignated from R.S. 30:2504 by Acts 2020, No. 317, § 2, eff. June 12, 2020. Acts 2018, No. 509, § 1. Amended by Acts 2019, No. 238, § 1; Acts 2022, No. 16, § 1; Acts 2022, No. 374, § 1.
