June 27, 2023

CPSB Meeting

 

Members present Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Barbara Smith-Iverson, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell. Also present were Dr. T. Lamar Goree, Secretary, and Mr. Reggie Abrams, Board Counsel.

 

President Dr. Terence Vinson called the June 27, 2023, meeting to order, and Board Member Mary Trammel gave the Invocation and led the Pledge of Allegiance.

 

CONSIDERATION OF THE MINUTES OF THE MAY 23, 2023, JUNE 6, 2023, and JUNE 13, 2023, CPSB MEETINGS. Ms. Trammel moved, seconded by Mrs. Yeates, to approve the minutes of the May 23, 2023, June 6, 2023, and June 13, 2023, CPSB meetings as submitted in the electronic mailout.  Vote on the motion carried unanimously.

 

PRESENTATIONS AND RECOGNITIONS. Ms. Marquel Sennet introduced the following presentations and recognitions, Caddo’s Director of Communications: 2023 Presidential Student Scholar; 2023 U.S. Presidential Distinguished Teacher-Scholar; 2023 Louisiana Teacher of the Year Finalist; 2023 National JAG Title Competition for Caddo Parish; Introduction of Caddo Emerging Leaders and Principal Interns for Spring 2023.

 

PUBLIC HEARING ON PROPOSED 2023-2024 CONSOLIDATED BUDGET.  Jon Glover, Shreveport, LA, had concerns about the ESSER Expenditures, and Kimberley Jones asked if Jon Glover’s questions would be answered.

 

VISITORS

The following visitors addressed the Board on matters of interest/concern:  Jon Glover asked the board to revisit how visitor comments were recorded in the minutes; Patsy Hamilton Soloman, Christela Barfield Jackson, Irma Rogers, Robert Williams, Karen White, Martha Glover, and Bobbie Young were all in support to the name change of Northside to Linear; Trevor Smith and Uneaka Jenkins were opposed to the name change of Northside to Linear; Kimberley Jones and James Talry concerned with the relocation of CVA to Green Oaks and asked the board to reconsider.

 

ESTABLISH THE AGENDA AND PROPOSED CONSENT AGENDA

Superintendent Goree highlighted items for the Board’s consideration.  President announced the following items are on consent: 6.02-6.04, 7.01, 8.02-8.03, and 13.01-13.02.

 

Mr. Rachal moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to establish the agenda and consent agenda for the June 27, 2023, CPSB meeting as presented.  Vote on the motion carried unanimously.

 

CONFIRM THE CONSENT AGENDA

Mr. Rachal moved, seconded by Dr. Douzart,  to confirm the consent agenda for the June 27, 2023 CPSB Meeting.  Vote on the motion carried, and the following is a summary of the Board’s action on the consent agenda items.

 

6.02 Personnel Leaves.  The Board approved the Personnel Leaves as recommended by the Superintendent and submitted in the electronic mailout

6.03 Personnel Resignations.  The Board approved the Personnel Resignations as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

6.04 Administrative Contract Renewals.  The Board approved the Administrative Contract Renewals as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

7.01 Consideration to Approve Capital Project Bids.The Board approved the bids as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout on the bid tabulation sheet: (1) Mackin Educational Resources – Library Books (2) Ferris Office Smart, LLC – Marker Boards and Bulletin Boards.

 

8.02 Consideration of the 2023-2024 Fiscal Year Official Journal. The Board approved The Shreveport Times as the 2023-2024 Fiscal Year Official Journal as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.03 Consideration to approve the amendment to the Cooperative Endeavor Agreement and Lease Agreement between Louisiana Tech University and Caddo Parish School Board to extend the lease term for use of the Caddo Virtual Academy facility from July 1, 2023-June 30, 2024.  The Board approved the amendment to the Cooperative Endeavor Agreement and Lease Agreement between Louisiana Tech University and Caddo Parish School Board to extend the lease term for the use of the Caddo Virtual Academy facility from July 1, 2023-June 30, 2024.

 

13.01 Student Readmission Appeal Hearings. The Board approved an appeal for student ZG as recommended by staff, whose parent is in agreement, and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

13.02 Request for Settlement Authority – Claimant: Zareah Douglas – Date of Loss 5/5/22 – Claim #4A22058VQKB-0001. The Board approved the settlement as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

8.04 Consideration of Proposed 2023-2024 Consolidated Annual Budget. After discussion, Mr. Rachal moved, seconded by Mrs. Bell, to approve the proposed 2023-2024 Consolidated Annual Budget as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout. The vote on the motion carried, with Mrs. McLain opposed. Ms. Smith-Iverson abstained.

 

8.05 To begin the process of (1) Changing the name of Northside Elementary School to Linear Middle School, the Mascot to Wildcats, and the colors to Red and White; and (2) Waiving the requirement in Policy FDC that a Public Hearing be held for community input at the regular monthly meeting immediately preceding the meeting that the School Board intends to act upon the Superintendent’s recommendation and instead allow community input at a Public Hearing to be held prior to the School Board’s vote on July 18, 2023, on the Superintendent’s recommendation. Ms. Green moved, seconded by Mrs. Bell, to begin the process of (1) Changing the name of Northside Elementary School to Linear Middle School, the Mascot to Wildcats, and the colors to Red and White; and (2) Waiving the requirement in Policy FDC that a Public Hearing be held for community input at the regular monthly meeting immediately preceding the meeting that the School Board intends to act upon the Superintendent’s recommendation and instead allow community input at a Public Hearing to be held prior to the School Board’s vote on July 18, 2023, on the Superintendent’s recommendation as recommended by Board Member Jasmine Green and submitted in the electronic mailout. After a lengthy discussion, Ms. Green moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to call for the question to end the discussion. The vote on the motion carried, with Mr. Umling and Mrs. Tharpe opposed, and the discussion ended. Ms. Green made a Substitute motion, seconded by Mrs. Bell to begin the process of (1) Changing the name of Northside Elementary School to Linear Middle School and (2) Waiving the requirement in Policy FDC that a Public Hearing be held for community input at the regular monthly meeting immediately preceding the meeting that the School Board intends to act upon the Superintendent’s recommendation and instead allow community input at a Public Hearing to be held prior to the School Board’s vote on July 18, 2023, on the Superintendent’s recommendation as recommended by Board Member Jasmine Green and submitted in the electronic mailout. The vote on the motion carried, with Mr. Umling, Mrs. Tharpe, Mrs. McLain, and Mrs. Yeates opposed. Dr. Vinson abstained.

 

8.06 Consideration of Authorizing the Superintendent to send a Letter of Support on behalf of the Caddo Parish School Board to the Port of Caddo-Bossier allowing D. E. Shaw Renewable Investments d/b/a Rocking R, Solar, LLC’s participation in the PILOT Program. Mr. Rachal moved, seconded by Ms. Smith-Iverson, to authorize the Superintendent to send a Letter of Support on behalf of the Caddo Parish School Board to the Port of Caddo- Bossier allowing D. E. Shaw Renewable Investments d/b/a Rocking R, Solar, LLC’s participation in the PILOT Program as submitted in the electronic mailout. The vote on the motion carried, with Mr. Umling opposed.  

 

ADJOURNMENT. Ms. Green moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to adjourn.  The vote on the motion carried, and the June 27, 2023, CPSB Meeting adjourned at approximately 7:15 p.m.
