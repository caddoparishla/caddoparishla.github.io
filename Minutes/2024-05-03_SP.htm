May 3, 2024

CPSB Special Session

 

The Caddo Parish School Board (CPSB) met in Special Session on May 3, 2024, at 4:30 p.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, Louisiana.  Board members present were Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr. Bonita

Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Jessica Yeates, Katie McLain, and Dottie Bell. Legal Counsel Reginald Abrams was also present.

1.01 Invocation (Dr. Bonita Douzart, District 5)

 

1.02 Pledge of Allegiance(Dr. Bonita Douzart, District 5)

 

1.03 Roll Call President Yeates called the meeting to order at approximately 4:30 p.m.

 

1.04 Visitors Director of Communications Marquel Sennet introduced our BESE Board Member, Stacey Melerine. Ms. Melerine gave opening remarks and introduced Dr. Cade Brumley, Louisiana Superintendent of Education. Dr. Brumley provided an overview of

the plan the Louisiana Department of Education recommended.

The following visitors were also present at the meeting: Danny Martin was opposed to the Third Future takeover of Fair Park; Mark Latterton requested more time before the decision was made about Fair Park; Quantavious Dowell was opposed to a takeover by a

charter school; Tatinisha Kochinskey was opposed to the Fair Park takeover; Gladys McKnight was opposed to the changes and requested Fair Park change back to a Medical Career Center; Roy Burrell was concerned about school closures and the repurposing

of the buildings; Cathy Bonds was concerned about the Fair Park takeover; Grant Rose was concerned with the closure of Sunset Acres and its impact on community; Shannon Ali was opposed to the closure of Sunset Acres and its effect on the community;

Rachael Haadad asked for more communication between teachers/central office and stated there were SPED teachers not certified teaching SPED; Craig Lee was opposed to the Fair Park takeover; Phillip Rozeman spoke in support of Westwood and Turner;

Jerry Page requested pay raise for teachers and asked the board to make the best decision for the students; Irma Rogers spoke on the takeover of Linear back in 2010 and stated COVID students were behind 2-3 years; Jackie Lansdale announced this was the

19th year of “The Most Improved Student” banquet and asked the board to bring hope to the students. She also asked the district to offer leave without pay to teachers moving to teach at charter schools; Jon Glover was concerned about the Werner Park and

Sunset Acres closures; Carol Smith reinforced the importance of Green Oaks Performing Arts Academy to the MLK Community.

 

1.05 Consideration to approve the recommendation of the Louisiana Department of Education and the Recovery School District plan as submitted in the electronic mailout, with the only change being that Superintendent Brumley has

approved Caddo Parish School Board’s use of Third Future Schools to operate Fair Park Middle as a Type 3 turnaround model for all grades beginning with the 2024-2025 school year.

Motion by Katie McLain, seconded by Don W. Little

Final Resolution: Motion Carries

Yes: Steve Umling, Don W. Little, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Christine Tharpe, Barry F. Rachal, Katie McClain, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell

No: Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Dr. Bonita Douzart

 

1.06 Adjournment Move to adjourn the May 3, 2024 CPSB Special Session

Motion by Don Little, second by Dottie Bell

Final Resolution: Motion Carries

Yes: Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell
