TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 39. THE CHILDREN FIRST ACT

PART I. GENERAL PROVISIONS

 

17:3871      Short title

   This Chapter may be referred to and may be cited as "The Children First Act".

Added by Acts 1988, No. 659, Sec. 1, eff. July 15, 1988.

PROVISIONAL EFFECT

         < Section 1 of Acts 1988, No. 659 amended R.S. 17:421.3 and enacted Chapter 39 of Title 17, "The Children First Act", including this section. Section 3 of Acts 1988, No. 659 provided in part: "If the provisions of R.S. 17:421.3 are not funded, the provisions of Chapter 39 of Title 17 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, as enacted in this Act, shall be null and void." >
