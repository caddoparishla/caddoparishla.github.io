TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 28. LOUISIANA UNIVERSITIES MARINE CONSORTIUM FOR RESEARCH AND EDUCATION

 

17:3451  Purpose

 

The purpose of this Chapter is to create the Louisiana Universities Marine Consortium for Research and Education as a statewide entity with the primary function of conducting research and promoting education in the marine sciences and marine technology, particularly where related to coastal resources and the impact of energy related industries upon these coastal resources.  Such functions shall be performed by the Louisiana Universities Marine Consortium on the Louisiana gulf coast.
