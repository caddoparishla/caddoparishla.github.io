October 1, 2024

CPSB Special Session

 

 

The Caddo Parish School Board (CPSB) met in Special Session on October 1, 2024, at 3:00 p.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, Louisiana.  Board members present were Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Jessica

Yeates, and Dottie Bell. Superintendent Keith Burton and Legal Counsel Reginald Abrams were also present.

1.01 Invocation (Mary Trammel, District 6)

1.02 Pledge of Allegiance (Mary Trammel, District 6)

 

1.03 Roll Call President Yeates called the meeting to order at approximately 3:00 p.m.

 

1.04 Visitors

Jon Glover feels the process for the applications and decision for the District 7 board seat is being rushed.

 

1.05 Declare District 7 Seat Vacant and Call for Special Election.

 

Move to approve a Proclamation declaring the District 7 Caddo Parish School Board seat, vacant due to the resignation of Darrin Dixon, and that CPSB call for a special election to fill said Vacancy as provided in said Proclamation.

Motion by Dottie Bell, seconded by Mary Trammel

Final Resolution: Motion Carried

Yes: Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell

 

1.06 Establish Procedures for Filling the District 7 Vacancy

 

Move that the following procedures for filling the District position be advertised in the official journal; That applications for filling the Caddo Parish School Board District 7 position be addressed to the President of the CPSB and accepted by hand delivery at the

Superintendent’s office located at 1961 Midway Avenue, Shreveport, LA or by email to dfeibel@caddoschools.org until 4:00 p.m. on Monday, October 14, 2024;

 

That applications contain the following:

1) The applicant’s date of birth;

2) The applicant’s address and how long they have lived in District 7; and

3) A short statement regarding why applicant desires to serve as interim board member from  District 7.

 

That interviews of candidates will follow the following format:

FORMAT FOR INTERVIEW SESSION – (Up to 10 minutes per candidate)

I. Each candidate will present an opening statement addressing the following:

A. His or her reasons for desiring to serve as interim board member from District 7;

B. His or her special skills or experience to offer as a board member from District 7; and

C. His or her perspective on the role of a board member.

II. Each candidate will be allowed to make any final summary statement if time permits.

Interviews to be held at the special board meeting to be held on October 15, 2024 at 2:30 p.m. at the Caddo Parish School Board office board room, 1961 Midway Avenue, Shreveport, LA.

Motion by Dottie Bell, seconded by Jasmine Green.

Final Resolution: Motion Carried

Yes: Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell

1.07 Call a Special Meeting to Interview Candidates and Select an Interim Appointment for the District 7 Vacancy.

 

Move that a special meeting be held on Tuesday, October 15, 2024, at 2:30 p.m. for the purpose of interviewing candidates for the District 7 Vacancy and for selecting an interim appointment.

Motion by Jasmine Green, seconded by Mary Trammel.

Final Resolution: Motion Carried

Yes: Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell

 

1.08 Adjournment

 

Move to adjourn the October 1, 2024 CPSB Special Session

Motion by Dottie Bell, seconded by Jasmine Green

Final Resolution:  Motion Carried

Yes:  Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell
