TITLE 23. LABOR AND WORKERS' COMPENSATION

CHAPTER 1. LOUISIANA WORKFORCE COMMISSION

PART IV. COMPREHENSIVE LABOR MARKET INFORMATION SYSTEM

 

23:74      Consumer information

 

     For the consumer information component of the system, there shall be a user-friendly formatted inventory of available training opportunities and, to the extent possible, employment opportunities.  This component of the system shall be available for access on the Internet.
