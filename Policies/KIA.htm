PUBLIC SOLICITATIONS IN THE SCHOOLS

 

 

Solicitation of students to promote the merit of any product by brand name or trademark, or the interests of any commercial or other non-school agency shall not be permitted by the Caddo Parish School Board.

 

The schools may, upon approval of the Superintendent, cooperate in furthering the work of any non-profit, non-discriminatory community-wide social service agency, provided that such cooperation does not restrict or impair the educational program of the schools.

 

Adopted:  September 16, 2014
