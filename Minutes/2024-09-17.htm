Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell.  Interim Superintendent Leisa G. Woolfolk and Board Counsel Reginald Abrams were also present.

12.01 Board Member Announcements

 

Mrs. Woolfolk introduced Don Gibbs, Director of Security. Mr. Gibbs shared information and updates regarding Security procedures in the district.  Mr. Little thanked Mr. Gibbs for his presentation, and stated to clear the record there was no plan to build a school

on Ellerbe Road.

Mr. Dixon announced he will submit his Letter of Resignation effective September 30, 2024 because he is relocating to Baton Rouge with his job.  Mr. Umling reflected on the year with Mr. Dixon on the board. Mr. Little, Ms .Trammel, and Mrs. Bell thanked Mr.

Dixon for his hard work and dedication while he served on the board. Dr. Douzart thanked Mr. Dixon for always being a gentleman.
