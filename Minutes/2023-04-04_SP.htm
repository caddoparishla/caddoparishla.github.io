April 4, 2023

CPSB Special Session

 

The Caddo Parish School Board (CPSB) met in Special Session on April 4, 2023, at 5:30 p.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, Louisiana.  Board members present were Steve Umling, Dr. Terence Vinson, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Barbara Smith-Iverson, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell.  Also present were Superintendent Lamar Goree and Legal Counsel Reginald Abrams.  

 

President Vinson called the meeting to order at approximately 5:30 p.m., and Ms. Trammel gave the Invocation and led the Pledge of Allegiance.  

 

Visitors

None

 

1.05 Capital Projects Approval of Bids – Mr. Rachal moved, seconded by Mrs. Bell, to approve the bids as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout on the bid tabulation sheets: 1) Project 2024-820, "Turner Health Clinic" be awarded to Hall Builders, Inc. (Not OCC*), Shreveport, LA, with a Base Bid, for the sum total of $325,675.00. The budget amount is $400,000.00. The project will be paid from Capital Projects Funds. 2) Project 2024-217, "South Highlands Campus Drainage," be awarded to A. Williams Enterprise, Inc. (Not OCC*), Shreveport, LA, with a Base Bid, for the sum total of $240,000.00. The budget amount is $250,000.00. The project will be paid from Capital Projects Funds. 3) Project 2024-225, "Northside Restroom Renovation,” be awarded to Daren Bailey, Inc. (Not OCC*), Shreveport, LA, with a Base Bid and Alternate 1, for the sum total of $289,367.00. The budget amount is $350,000.00. The project will be paid from Capital Projects Funds.  The vote on the motion carried unanimously.

 

Adjournment.  Mrs. Smith-Iverson moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to adjourn.  The vote on the motion carried, with Mrs. McLain, Mrs. Yeates, and Mrs. Bell opposed. The meeting adjourned at approximately 5:45 p.m.
