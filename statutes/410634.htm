TITLE 41. PUBLIC LANDS

CHAPTER 6. SCHOOL LANDS

PART I. IN GENERAL

 

41:634        Issuance of scrip in certain cases

 When the locations can not be made, if deemed more advantageous to the state, the register, with the assent of the federal government, may issue scrip for the lands, which scrip shall not be sold for a less amount than one dollar and twenty-five cents per acre.
