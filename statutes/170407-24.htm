TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 1. GENERAL SCHOOL LAW

PART X-A. LOUISIANA EARLY CHILDHOOD EDUCATION

 

17:407.24  Rules

 

The State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education shall promulgate rules and regulations in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act to implement the provisions of this Part.
