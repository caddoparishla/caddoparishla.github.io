March 19, 2024

CPSB Meeting

 

Members presentSteve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell. Dr. T. Lamar Goree, Secretary, and Mr. Reggie Abrams, Board Counsel, were also present.

 

President Jessica Yeates called March 19, 2024, meeting to order. Board Member Dr. Terence Vinson gave the Invocation, and a North Highlands Elementary School student led the Pledge of Allegiance.

 

CONSIDERATION OF THE MINUTES OF THE FEBRUARY 6, 2024, FEBRUARY 12, 2024, AND FEBRUARY 20, 2024 CPSB MEETINGS. Ms. Trammel moved, seconded by Mr. Little, to approve the minutes of the February 6, 2024, February 12, 2024, and February 20, 2024, CPSB meetings as submitted in the electronic mailout.  Vote on the motion carried unanimously.

 

PRESENTATIONS AND RECOGNITIONS. Ms. Marquel Sennet, Director of Communications, introduced Caddo Middle Magnet 6th grader Lacee Smith. Lacee sang “I’m Here” from the Broadway musical The Color Purple. For the second year in a row, the Northwood High School’s JROTC Rifle Team is the Army JROTC State Champions. Ms. Sennet congratulated Army Top Shooter Rylee Beldin and the other team shooters, Cobie Miller, Ian Anthony, Michelle Wolsey, Kaylee Maples, Zoey Chapman, and Charles Chadsey.   Ms. Sennet recognized Maureen Barclay of Captain Shreve High School as the 2024 Louisiana School Librarian of the Year. She added that the honor is a testament to her dedication and contributions to education and library sciences.  Ms. Sennet stated that Caddo has long been the leader in our region for students achieving the distinction of National Merit Finalists.  The 2023-2024 National Merit Finalists are:  Caddo Parish Magnet High School-Vasu Arora, Sophie Chen, Hailey Huguet, Ruby Knoebel, Logan O’Callaghan, Marshall O’Callaghan, Jack Randall, Mason Sater, Logan Tais, Anjali Veerareddy, Phoebe Voumard, Guangjun (Tom) Wang; Captain Shreve High School-Robert Auchard and Reynard Landreneau; C. E. Byrd High School-Hudson Kate Roberts.

 

VISITORS The following visitors addressed the Board: Jon Glover voiced her concern that committee meetings are not live-streamed and stated backup documentation should be available to the public other than constituents accessing Board Docs; Jordan Thomas expressed her opposition to the Legislative decisions being made and explained all funds should remain in Caddo Schools; Latitia Burgh voiced her concern of the audits of Queensborough and Werner Park;  Marvin Muhammed spoke in support of Green Oaks Performing Academy; Lola Lawson spoke in support of Green Oaks Performing Academy;  Carol Smith spoke in support of Green Oaks Performing Academy; Jerry Moore spoke in support of Green Oaks Performing Academy.

 

ESTABLISH THE AGENDA AND PROPOSED CONSENT AGENDA Superintendent Goree highlighted items for the Board’s consideration.  President Yeates announced the following items are on consent: 6.01-6.03, 7.02, 8.01-8.02, 8.04-8.05, 13.01, 13.03-13.04.

 

Mrs. Bell moved, seconded by Ms. Green, to establish the agenda and consent agenda for the March 19, 2024, CPSB meeting as presented. The vote on the motion carried unanimously.

 

CONFIRM THE CONSENT AGENDA Mr. Dixon moved, seconded by Mrs. Bell, to confirm the consent agenda for the March 19, 2024, CPSB Meeting.  The vote on the motion carried, and the following is a summary of the Board’s action on the consent agenda items.

 

6.01 Personnel Recommendations. The Board approved Personnel Recommendations as recommended by the Superintendent and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

6.02 Personnel Leaves. The Board approved Personnel Leaves as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

6.03 Personnel Resignations.  The Board approved the Personnel Resignations as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

7.01 Capital Project Bids. The Board approved the bids as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout on the bid tabulation sheets: 1) Project 2025-225 "Northside Gym Renovation" be awarded to Martin Construction & Design (OCC*), Shreveport, LA, with a Base Bid, Alternate 1, Alternate 2 and Alternate 3, for the sum total of $312,170.00. The budget amount is $400,000.00. The project will be paid for with Capital Projects Funds. 2) Project 2025-224 "Green Oaks Gym Renovation" be awarded to Martin Construction & Design (OCC*), Shreveport, LA, with a Base Bid, Alternate 1 and Alternate 2, for the sum total of 451,026.00. The budget amount is $450,000.000. The project will be paid for with Capital Project Funds 3) Project 2025-226 "Turner Gym Renovation" be awarded to Martin Construction & Design (OCC*), Shreveport, LA, with a Base Bid for the sum total of $262,262.00. The budget amount is $500,000.00. The project will be paid for with Capital Projects Funds.

 

7.02 Purchasing Bids.  The Board approved the following bids as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout on the bid tabulation sheets: (1) Shreveport-Bossier Astronomical Society, Inc. - 04L-24-Lease– Worley Observatory Property.

 

8.01 Property/Bus Rental Requests. The Board approved the Property/Bus rental requests as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.02 Consideration of Resolution of Continued Employment. The Board approved Resolution of Continued Employment as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.04 Consideration to authorize staff to obtain an appraisal and title for the Whitehall Property. The Board authorized staff to obtain an appraisal and title for approximately a 0.93-acre tract of land with intent to list for sale at the appraised amount with our Listing Agent from U. L. Coleman, Inc. – Section 20, Township 15 West, Range 15 North, Caddo Parish, Louisiana (Whitehall Property – land only) as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.  

 

8.05 Consideration to approve an amendment to the Lease Agreement between CPSB and Parish of Caddo.  The Board approved an amendment to the Lease Agreement between CPSB and Parish of Caddo for PBS Pinchback Park, located at 7903 Jefferson Paige Road, for additional space to expand public parking to accommodate a future community center where the park is located as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

13.01 Student Readmission Appeal. The Board approved appeal for students TH and MM as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout – Parents/Guardians are in agreement.

 

13.03 Request for Settlement Authority-Claimant Name: Marcus Sudds – Date of Loss: 05/02/2017- Claim #6472878. The Board approved the Request for Settlement Authority-Claimant Name: Marcus Sudds–Date of Loss:05/02/2017-Claim #6472878 as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

13.04 Request for Settlement Authority- Claimant Name: Lydia Rodgers—Date of Loss: 09/23/2022—Claim #4A2209T7J27. The Board approved the Request for Settlement Authority- Claimant Name: Lydia Rodgers—Date of Loss: 09/23/2022—Claim #4A2209T7J27 as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.03 Consideration to authorize staff to begin working with Southern University at Shreveport to create a joint proposal for the creation of the Newton Smith Center for Workforce Solutions. Ms. Green moved, seconded by Mrs. Bell, to authorize staff to begin working with Southern University at Shreveport to create a joint proposal for the creation of Newton Smith Center for Workforce Solutions, as submitted in the electronic mailout.  The vote on the motion carried, and Dr. Vinson abstained.

 

Executive Session

Ms. Trammel moved, seconded by Mr. Rachal, to Executive Session. The vote on the motion carried, with Mrs. McLain not present at the vote. The Board met in Executive Session for 10 minutes, then reconvened in open session.

 

13.05 Request for Settlement Authority-Claimant Name: Caddo Parish School Board, and parties American Contractors Indemnity Company, Henderson Construction Services, Inc., Reliance Plumbing Group, Inc. – Captain Shreve Restroom Project No.2023-237. Mr. Dixon moved, seconded by Mr. Umling, to approve the Request for Settlement Authority in the claim regarding Claimant-Caddo Parish School Board and Parties American Contractors Indemnity Company, Henderson Construction Services., Reliance Plumbing Group, Inc. on Project No. 2023-237 Captain Shreve Restroom, as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.  The vote on the motion carried, with Mrs. McLain not present at vote.

 

Announcements. Mrs. McLain announced the birth of her son. Dr. Vinson thanked those in education for their hard work and dedication. Mr. Rachal announced he was the Texas winner of the shootout competition from this past weekend. Mrs. Bell added that Mrs. McLain recently gave birth and brought the baby to the meeting. Thank you, Mrs. McLain, for your dedication.   Mr. Dixon said he visited several schools and enjoyed the visits.  Mr. Umling shared information regarding a gun safety course through the Sheriff’s Department.

 

Adjournment Ms. Trammel moved, seconded by Ms. Green, to adjourn the March 19, 2024 CPSB Meeting.  The vote on the motion carried, with Mrs. McLain not present at vote. The meeting was adjourned at 5:45 p.m.
