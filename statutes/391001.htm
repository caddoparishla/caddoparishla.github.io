TITLE 39. PUBLIC FINANCE

SUBTITLE II. LOCAL FINANCE

CHAPTER 4. BONDED INDEBTEDNESS AND SPECIAL TAXES

PART XII. BONDS TO ACQUIRE PLANT SITES

 

39:1001        Part as full authority

This Part shall constitute full authority for the accomplishment of all acts herein authorized to be done.  No other law restricting the carrying out of the acts authorized to be done shall be construed as applying to any proceedings had or acts done pursuant to this Part.
