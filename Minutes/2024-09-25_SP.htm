Caddo Parish School Board Special Session
September 25, 2024

 

 

The Caddo Parish School Board met in Special Session on Wednesday, September 25, 2024, at 1:30 p.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, LA. The Board Members present were Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr.

Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell. Interim Superintendent Leisa Woolfolk and Board Counsel Reggie Abrams were also present.

 

1.01 Invocation - Barry Rachal, District 9

 

1.02 Pledge of Allegiance - Barry Rachal, District 9

 

1.03 Roll Call - President Yeates called the meeting to order at 1:30 p.m.

 

1.04 Visitors - Craig Lee, Willie Bradford, Marvin Muhammad, Jon Glover, Jackie Lansdale, Kimberlea Jones, and Curtis Hooks.

 

1.05 Report on Contract Negotiations

Mr. Abrams announced the proposed Contract that was sent to the board on Monday stated the following: 4-year Contract beginning October 1, 2024, through September 30, 2028; base salary of $260,000.00 with a 2% annual increase provided the

Superintendent met or exceeded expectations according to his evaluation; $20,000.00 performance incentive based on evidence of growth in relation to meeting or exceeding the goals in the five areas provided; entitled to any stipend provided but would not

increase base salary. Board Member McLain asked for a hard copy of the Contract. President Yeates called for a 5-minute recess. After the 5-minute recess, President Yeates announced that the meeting was back in session. The discussion continued regarding

the contract negotiations.  

 

1.06 Executive Session, if necessary, to discuss the character and/or professional competence of Superintendent Candidate – Not necessary

 

1.07 Elect and Announce the Superintendent of Caddo Parish Public Schools and Approve Contract

 

Motion 1

Move to elect Keith Burton as Superintendent for a 4-year term beginning October 1, 2024, and authorize the Board President to execute the proposed Contract.

Motion by Christine Tharpe, second by Katie McLain

 

Motion 2

Substitute Motion:

Move to elect Keith Burton contingent upon contract negotiations

Motion by Don Little, second by Barry Rachal

 

President Yeates called for a 15-minute recess. After the 15-minute recess, President Yeates announced the meeting was back in session. Board Member Little withdrew his substitute motion, and Board Member Rachal withdrew his second to Mr. Little’s

substitute motion. Mrs. Tharpe called for the question to end the discussion.

 

Call for the question to end the discussion on the original motion

Motion by Christine Tharpe, second by Don W Little.

Motion Fails

Yes: Steve Umling, Don Little, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell

No: Jasmine Green, Terence Vinson, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Katie McLain

Not Present at Vote: Dr. Bonita Douzart

 

The motion failed, and the discussion continued. President Yeates called for a 5-minute recess for Attorney Abrams to consult with Mr. Burton regarding contract negotiations. After the 5-minute recess, President Yeates announced the meeting was back in

session. Mr. Abrams stated Mr. Burton agreed with the following changes to his Contract: 3-year Contract beginning October 1, 2024, through September 30, 2027; base salary reduced from $260,000.00 to $255,000.00 with a 2% annual increase for meeting or

exceeding expectations according to his evaluation; increase performance incentive from $20,000.00 to $25,000.00 based on evidence of growth in relation to meeting or exceeding the goals in the five areas provided; eligibility for stipends remain the same. After

discussion, as the maker of the original motion, Mrs. Tharpe accepted the contract negotiation changes. As the board member who seconded Mrs. Tharpe’s original motion, Mrs. McLain accepted the contract negotiations. 

 

Original Motion #1 with contract negotiations as presented

 

Move to elect Keith Burton as Superintendent for a 4-year term beginning October 1, 2024, and authorize the Board President to execute the proposed Contract with the following changes: Change from a 4-year contract to a 3-year contract, decrease salary from

$260K to $255K, and increase incentives to $25K from $20K.

Motion by Christine Tharpe, second by Katie McLain.

Final Resolution: Motion Carries

Yes: Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Don Little, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell

No: Dr. Terence Vinson, Darrin Dixon

Not Present at Vote: Dr. Bonita Douzart
