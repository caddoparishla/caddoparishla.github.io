Title 17. Education

Chapter 1. General School Law

Part X-B. Early Learning Center Licensing

 

17:407.52      Coordination

 

The department shall coordinate with the office of state fire marshal and the Department of Health and Hospitals to align standards for licensing of early learning centers with the standards for early childhood education programs.
