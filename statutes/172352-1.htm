TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 12. SCIENTIFIC RESEARCH

PART II. ANATOMICAL GIFT ACT

 

17:2352.1      Coordination of procurement and use

 

Each hospital in this state shall enter into an agreement or affiliation with procurement organizations for the coordination of procurement and use of anatomical gifts.
