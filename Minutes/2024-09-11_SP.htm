September 11, 2024

CPSB Special Session

 

 

The Caddo Parish School Board (CPSB) met in Special Session on September 11, 2024 at 9:00 a.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, LA.  Board Members present were Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr. Bonita

Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell.  Also present were Interim Superintendent Leisa G. Woolfolk and Board Counsel Reggie Abrams

1.05 Executive Session - Interview Candidate, Keith Burton, for the Position of Superintendent of Caddo Parish Public Schools

 

Move into Executive Session to interview candidate, Keith Burton, for the Position of Superintendent of Caddo Parish Public Schools

Motion by Don Little, second by Barry Rachal.

Final Resolution: Motion Fails

Yes: Steve Umling, Don Little, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Jessica Yeates

No: Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Katie McLain, Dottie Bell

Yes: Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Dottie Bell

No: Steve Umling, Don Little, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates

 

The interview of candidate, Keith Burton, for the position of Superintendent of Caddo Parish Public School, was conducted in open session.
