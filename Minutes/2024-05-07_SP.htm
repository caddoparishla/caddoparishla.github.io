May 7, 2024

CPSB Special Session

 

 

The Caddo Parish School Board (CPSB) met in Special Session on May 7, 2024, at 4:30 p.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, Louisiana.  Board members present were Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr. Bonita

Douzart, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell.  Legal Counsel Reginald Abrams was also present.

1.01 Invocation (Dr. Bonita Douzart, District 5)

1.02 Pledge of Allegiance (Dr. Bonita Douzart, District 5)

 

1.03 Roll Call President Yeates called the meeting to order at approximately 4:30 p.m.

 

1.04 Visitors Ms. Jon Glover had concerns regarding the receiving schools for the students from Werner Park and Sunset Acres being D/F Schools.

 

1.05 Capital Project Bids Move to approve the bids as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout on the bid tabulation sheets: 1) Project 2025-243: "Keithville Sewage Lift Station and Retention Ponds Repair" be Revised and Rebid 2) Project

2025-244: "Northwood Track Resurfacing" be awarded to Hellas Construction (Not OCC*), Austin, TX, with Base Bid, for the sum total of $444,200.00. The budget amount is $500,000.00. The project will be paid for with Capital Projects Funds. 3) Project 2025

219: "South Highlands Canopy Roofing" be awarded to ELA Group (Not OCC*), Shreveport, LA, with a Base Bid for the sum total of $294,500.00. The budget amount is $350,000.00. The project will be paid for with Capital Projects Funds.

Motion by Mary Trammel, second by Dottie Bell.

Final Resolution: Motion Carries

Yes: Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don W Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Barry F Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell

 

1.06 Consideration to approve the proposed Attendance Zones for Werner Park Elementary and Sunset Acres Elementary Move to approve the proposed Attendance Zones beginning with the 2024-2025 school year as recommended by staff and

submitted in the electronic mailout: Werner Park Elementary students will attend Caddo Heights Math/Science Elementary, and Sunset Acres Elementary students will attend Westwood Elementary.

Motion by Christine Tharpe, second by Barry F Rachal.

Final Resolution: Motion Carries

Yes: Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Barry F Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell

No: Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Dr. Bonita Douzart

Not Present at Vote: Don W Little

 

Move to call for the question to end the discussion

Motion by Christine Tharpe, second by Mary Trammel.

Motion Carries

Yes: Steve Umling, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Barry F Rachal, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell

No: Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Katie McLain

Not Present at Vote: Don W Little

 

1.07 Adjournment Move to adjourn the May 7, 2024 CPSB Special Session.

Motion by Dottie Bell, second by Jasmine Green.

Final Resolution: Motion Carries

Yes: Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Barry F Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell
