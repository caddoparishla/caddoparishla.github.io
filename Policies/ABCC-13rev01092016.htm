TERM OF OFFICE

 

 

All members of the Caddo Parish School Board shall serve for four-year concurrent terms.  School Board members shall be elected at the same time as members of the United States Congress are elected to office.

 

The term of each member shall begin on January 1, following his/her election and expire on December 31, four (4) years later.  Each member of the School Board elected after January 1, 2014 shall be limited to serve no more than three (3) consecutive four-year terms.

 

Approved:  February 23, 2016

 

 

Ref:    La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §§17:52, 17:60.4, 17:71.2, 17:71.3
