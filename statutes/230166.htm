TITLE 23. LABOR AND WORKERS' COMPENSATION

CHAPTER 3. EMPLOYMENT STANDARDS AND CONDITIONS

PART I. MINORS

SUBPART B. EMPLOYMENT PRIVILEGES AND RESTRICTIONS

 

23:166      Minors fourteen and fifteen years of age; employments permitted

 Minors fourteen and fifteen years of age may be employed in any gainful occupation not prohibited in this Part, only after school hours and during non-school days.
