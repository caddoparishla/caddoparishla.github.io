TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 20-B. LOANS FOR STUDENTS ENROLLED IN COLLEGIATE TEACHER PREPARATION PROGRAMS

 

17:3042.5      Funding

  The commission may receive and accept funds from public and private sources to meet administrative and other expenses of the implementation of this Chapter.

Acts 1986, No. 583, Sec. 1, eff. July 2, 1986. Amended by Acts 1992, No. 984, Sec. 6.
