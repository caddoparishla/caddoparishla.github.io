June 4, 2024

CPSB Special Session

 

 

The Caddo Parish School Board (CPSB) met in Special Session on June 4, 2024, at 4:30 p.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, Louisiana.  Board members present were Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr. Bonita

Douzart, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell.  Interim Superintendent, Leisa G. Woolfolk and Legal Counsel Reginald Abrams were also present.

1.01 Invocation (Mary Trammel, District 6)

1.02 Pledge of Allegiance (Mary Trammel, District 6)

 

1.03 Roll Call President Yeates called the meeting to order at approximately 4:29 p.m.

 

1.04 Visitors None

 

1.05 Consideration to approve the recommendation of McPherson & Jacobson, LLC as the Superintendent Recruitment Service

Move to approve the recommendation of McPherson & Jacobson, LLC as the Superintendent Recruitment Services as submitted in the electronic mailout.

Motion by Darrin Dixon, second by Mary Trammel.

Final Resolution: Motion Carried

Yes: Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Don W Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Christine Tharpe, Barry F Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell

No: Dr. Terence Vinson

Move to adjourn the June 4, 2024 CPSB Special Session.

Final Resolution: Motion Carried
