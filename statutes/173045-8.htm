TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 20-E. LOUISIANA OPPORTUNITY LOAN PROGRAM

 

17:3045.8  Loan funds;  availability

 

The receipt of a LA-OP loan by an eligible borrower is subject to the availability of lending capital provided by appropriation.
