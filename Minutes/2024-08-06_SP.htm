August 6, 2024

CPSB Special Session

 

 

The Caddo Parish School Board (CPSB) met in Special Session on August 6, 2024, at 4:30 p.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, Louisiana.  Board members present were Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don W. Little, Dr.

Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Christine Tharpe, Barry F. Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell.  Interim Superintendent Leisa G. Woolfolk and Legal Counsel Reginald Abrams were also present.

1.01 Invocation (Christine Tharpe, District 8)

1.02 Pledge of Allegiance (Christine Tharpe, District 8)

 

1.03 Roll Call President Yeates called the meeting to order at approximately 4:30 p.m.

 

1.04 Visitors Jon Glover asked why the school board would not agree to give all employees an increase in salary instead of just stipends. Jackie Lansdale stated she commends the board for approving the employee stipend. Sheila Wimberly expressed her

concern about the superintendent search. She said she had already heard names and asked the board to be open to all choices.

 

1.05 Consideration to approve Cooperative Endeavor Agreement between Caddo Parish School Board and the City of Shreveport regarding School Zone Speed Enforcement Camera Revenue Move to approve Cooperative Endeavor Agreement

between Caddo Parish School Board and the City of Shreveport regarding School Zone Speed Enforcement Camera Revenue as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

Motion by Mary Trammel, second by Darrin Dixon.

Final Resolution: Motion Carries

Yes: Steve Umling, Dr. Terence Vinson, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Christine Tharpe, Barry F. Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell

Abstain: Don W. Little

 

1.06 Consideration to approve the Distribution Plan for the State One-time Stipend to Employees Move to approve the Distribution Plan for the State one-time Stipend to Employees as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

Motion by Darrin Dixon, second by Mary Trammel.

Final Resolution: Motion Carries

Yes: Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don W. Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Christine Tharpe, Barry F. Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell

 

1.07 Consideration to approve a one-time Back-to-School Stipend Move to approve a one-time back-to-school stipend as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

Motion by Dottie Bell, second by Dr. Terence Vinson

Final Resolution: Motion Carries

Yes: Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don W. Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Christine Tharpe, Barry F. Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell

 

1.08 Consideration to approve revisions of Policy IHA-Grading Systems Move to approve revisions of Policy IHA as recommended by Board Member Umling based on the Legislative Session and submitted in the electronic mailout.

Motion by: Steve Umling, second by Mary Trammel

Final Resolution: Motion Carries

Yes: Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Terence Vinson, Don W. Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Christine Tharpe, Barry F. Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell

 

1.09 Adjournment Move to adjourn the August 6, 2024 CPSB Special Session.

Motion by Dottie Bell, second by Mary Trammel

Final Resolution: Motion Carries

Yes: Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don W. Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Christine Tharpe, Barry F. Rachal, Katie McClain, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell
