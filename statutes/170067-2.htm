TITLE 17. EDUCATION

 CHAPTER 1. GENERAL SCHOOL LAW

 PART II. PARISH SCHOOL BOARDS

 SUBPART A. ESTABLISHMENT AND ORGANIZATION

 

17:67.2    Redistribution of minimum foundation program to other school systems

 

<Section effective upon approval of proposed constitutional amendment by Act 295>

 

To ensure that the creation of the Southeast Baton Rouge Community School District does not have a negative impact on the minimum foundation program allocation to school systems, beginning in the fiscal year immediately following the effective date of this Act and continuing for the next four fiscal years, the State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education shall identify the districts in parishes other than East Baton Rouge on which the creation of the Southeast Baton Rouge Community School System causes a negative impact and determine the amount of the negative impact on the minimum foundation per pupil allocation.  The State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education shall withhold the total of the amounts of the negative impact on all districts in parishes other than East Baton Rouge from the total minimum foundation program allocation to the Southeast Baton Rouge Community School District and transfer those funds on a per pupil basis to such districts.
