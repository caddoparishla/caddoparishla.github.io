July 20, 2023

CPSB Special Session

 

The Caddo Parish School Board (CPSB) met in Special Session on July 20, 2023, at 3:30 p.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, Louisiana.  Board members present were Steve Umling, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell.  Also present were Superintendent Lamar Goree and Legal Counsel Reginald Abrams.  

 

President Vinson called the meeting to order at approximately 3:30 p.m., and Ms. Trammel gave the Invocation and led the Pledge of Allegiance.  

 

Visitors

Brandy Pou announced her support for Candidate Darrin Dixon; Jon Glover stated that the person appointed by the Board to fill the District 7 Vacancy needed to get the job done.

 

1.05 Interview Candidates Interested in Filling the District 7 Vacancy The two candidates, Mr. Darrin Dixon and Ms. Linda Rasberry Smith, hand-delivered their applications to fill the Caddo Parish School Board District 7 position to the Superintendent’s office located at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, LA, before the deadline of 4:00 p.m. on Wednesday, July 19, 2023. According to the interview format, Mr. Dixon and Ms. Smith were given ten minutes each to present statements that addressed the following: reasons for desiring to serve as the interim board member from District 7, special skills or experience to offer as a board member from District 7, perspective on the role of a board member. Each candidate made a final summary statement.

 

1.06 Selection of Interim Representative to Fill District 7 Vacancy   President Vinson announced he will call each candidate’s name alphabetically, and each board member will have one vote.  As each candidate’s name was announced, the following votes were cast: (1) Darrin Dixon – 8 votes (Umling, Vinson, Little, Trammel, Tharpe, McLain, Yeates, Bell); (2) Linda Rasberry Smith – 1 vote (Douzart); Board members Green and Rachal were absent.

1.09 Administer Oath of Office for Interim Appointment to Fill District 7 Vacancy Legal Counsel Reginald Abrams administered the oath of office to Darrin Dixon, newly appointed interim board member for CPSB District 7.  The board and audience extended congratulations and welcomed Mr. Dixon to the board.

Adjournment.  Dr. Vinson moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to adjourn.  The vote on the motion carried, and the meeting adjourned at approximately 4:10 p.m.
