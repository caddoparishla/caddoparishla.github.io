TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 24-A. PROPRIETARY SCHOOLS

 

17:3141.7      Court appeal

 Any applicant for a license who is dissatisfied with the ruling of the Board of Regents after public hearing may file a written appeal to the district court for the parish in which the applicant resides within ninety days after notice of the final determination of the Board of Regents. Written notice of the appeal shall be served on the presiding officer of the Board of Regents, stating the reasons therefor. The decision of the district court shall be final.
