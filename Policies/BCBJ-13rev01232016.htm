BROADCASTING AND TAPING OF SCHOOL BOARD MEETINGS

 

 

It shall be the policy of the Caddo Parish School Board that all regular and special meetings shall be open to, and may be recorded by the public and representatives of the news media, television, and radio.  Proper decorum shall be maintained during all School Board meetings.  The operators of broadcasting and video equipment, cameras and lighting shall position themselves in the front of the School Board meeting room during official School Board meetings.  Operators should refrain from stationing themselves and/or their equipment on the School Board’s platform after proceedings are underway.

 

Adopted:  February 23, 2016

 

 

Ref:    La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §§17:81, 42:23
