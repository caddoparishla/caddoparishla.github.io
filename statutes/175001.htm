Title 17. Education

Chapter 50. Taylor Opportunity Program for Students

Part I. General

Subpart A. Awards

 

17:5001      Taylor Opportunity Program for Students

 

As part of the Louisiana Taylor Opportunity Program for Students, the state shall provide financial assistance to any eligible student who enrolls on a full-time basis in an eligible college or university to pursue an academic undergraduate degree or skill or occupational training as defined by the administering agency including a vocational or technical education certificate or diploma program or a nonacademic undergraduate degree.
