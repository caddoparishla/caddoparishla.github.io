Title 17. Education

Chapter 50. Taylor Opportunity Program for Students

Part II. Administration


17:5061       Administering agency

 

The provisions of this Chapter shall be administered by the Board of Regents. The administering agency shall provide by rule adopted as provided by the Administrative Procedure Act for all matters necessary to the implementation of this Chapter.
