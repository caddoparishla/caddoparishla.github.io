October 15, 2024

CPSB Special Session

 

 

The Caddo Parish School Board (CPSB) met in Special Session on October 15, 2024, at 2:30 p.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, Louisiana.  Board members present were Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr. Bonita

Douzart, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell. Superintendent Keith Burton and Legal Counsel Reginald Abrams were also present.

1.01 Invocation (Don Little, District 4)

1.02 Pledge of Allegiance (Don Little, District 4)

 

1.03 Roll Call President Yeates called the meeting to order at approximately 2:30 p.m.

 

1.04 Visitors

Jon Glover asked the board to make sure that the person they choose as interim board member for District 7 is ready to give all they have to the boys and girls in their district.

Rev. Joe Gant, Jr. spoke on behalf of applicant Pamela Jackson and stated he encouraged her to run for this interim seat. He said she was a master teacher in DeSoto and Caddo Parishes works in his church, and she loves the Lord and children.

 

1.05 Interview Candidates Interested in Filling District 7 Vacancy

 

The candidates presented, in alphabetical order (Jerry Bowman, Jr., Carla Buntyn, Pamela Jackson, Linda Rasberry Smith), an opening statement that addressed the following:

 

reasons for desiring to serve as interim board member from District 7, special skills or experience to offer as board members from District 7, and perspective on the role of the board member.

 

1.06 Selection of Interim Representative to Fill District 7 Vacancy

 

The board members voted on the candidate of their choice.  Mr. Jerry Bowman, Jr. received the most votes.

 

Jerry Bowman, Jr.

Yes: Steve Umling, Dr. Terence Vinson, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell

 

Carly Buntyn

Yes: Dr. Bonita Douzart, Katie McLain

 

Pamela M. Jackson

Yes: 0

 

Linda Rasberry Smith: 0

 

1.07 Administer Oath of Office for Interim Appointment to Fill District 7 Vacancy

 

Mr. Reginald Abrams, Board Counsel, administrated the Oath of Office for the Interim Appointment to fill the Caddo Parish School Board District 7 vacancy. Mr. Jerry Bowman, Jr. accepted the appointment and took the Oath of Office to serve as the Interim

Caddo Parish School Board member for District 7.

 

1.08 Adjournment

 

Move to adjourn the October 15, 2024 CPSB Special Session

Motion by Jessica Yeates, second by Dottie Bell

Final Resolution: Motion Carries

Yes: Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell
