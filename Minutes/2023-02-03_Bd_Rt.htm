February 3, 2023

CPSB Retreat

 

The Caddo Parish School Board (CPSB) held a retreat beginning at 9:00 a.m. on Friday, February 3, 2023, at the American Rose Center.  Those in attendance were Board members Steve Umling, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Mary Trammel, Barbara Smith-Iverson, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell.  Dr. Lamar Goree, Superintendent, Reggie Abrams, Board Counsel, and Darla Feibel, Secretary to the Board, were also in attendance. 

 

Superintendent Goree welcomed Board members to the retreat and thanked them for taking the time out of their schedules to attend.  He highlighted the agenda for the day. Dr. Goree facilitated a leadership exercise to encourage the Board Members to dig into the rationale behind their decision to enter public service and the importance of building a team of 12 Board members to serve the needs of students.  Then Dr. Goree initiated an activity that reviewed how to identify the most common dysfunctions, specifically within the Caddo Parish School Board, and how to overcome those dysfunctions to become a highly productive Board. The Superintendent gave an overview of the powers, duties, and responsibilities of School Boards and asked the Board Members to reflect on the “why” they have chosen the area of public service in education.  Mr. Abrams reviewed the Louisiana Ethics laws, the Caddo Parish School Board Code of Ethics, and a review of Board policies which outlined the means of operations of the Board and the district.

 

2023 Board Priorities Dr. Goree asked the Board to reflect on the five top priorities they hope to accomplish during their term. The following staff members addressed each priority that was relevant to their position: (1) Keith Burton, Chief Academic Officer (2) Leisa Woolfolk, Chief Human Resource Officer (3) Patrick Greer, Chief Operations Officer (4) Jeff Howard, Chief Financial Officer. 

 

Adjournment The Caddo Parish School Board Retreat adjourned at approximately 1:30 p.m.
