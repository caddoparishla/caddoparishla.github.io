SUPERINTENDENT QUALIFICATIONS

 

 

The Caddo Parish School Board shall elect a Superintendent for the School District who has met or has been certified as meeting the minimum requirements for Superintendency Certification as set by the Louisiana Board of Elementary and Secondary Education (BESE).  Additional qualifications may be stipulated by the School Board.

 

Applicants for the position of Superintendent shall be required to provide assurance that certification for the position is current and valid.

 

Approved:  February 23, 2016
