November 20, 2023

CPSB Special Session

 

The Caddo Parish School Board (CPSB) met in Special Session on November 20, 2023, at 4:30 p.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, Louisiana.  Board members present were Steve Umling, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell.  Also present were Superintendent Lamar Goree and Legal Counsel Reginald Abrams.  

 

President Vinson called the meeting to order at approximately 4:30 p.m., and Mrs. Dottie Bell gave the Invocation and led the Pledge of Allegiance.  

 

Visitors

None

 

1.05 Consideration to reschedule the November 21, 2023, CPSB Meeting to Tuesday, November 28, 2023 at 4:30 p.m. Mr. Dixon moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to approve rescheduling the November 21, 2023, CPSB Meeting to Tuesday, November 28, 2023, at 4:30 p.m.  The vote on the motion carried, and Mr. Little abstained. 

 

Adjournment. Ms. Trammel moved, seconded by Mr. Umling, to adjourn.  The vote on the motion carried, and the meeting adjourned at approximately 4:45 p.m.
