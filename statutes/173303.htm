TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 26. COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES

PART IV. ORGANIZATION

 

17:3303        Heads of public colleges and universities

There shall be a head of each public college and university who shall be appointed by its governing board taking into consideration the recommendations of the president or chief executive officer of the system.  The board shall adopt an appropriate title by which each head shall be designated.  The head of each public college and university shall serve at the pleasure of the board at a salary fixed by the board.
