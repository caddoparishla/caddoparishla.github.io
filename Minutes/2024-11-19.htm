November 19, 2024

CPSB Meeting

 

The Caddo Parish School Board met on November 19, 2024 at 4:30 p.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, LA. Board members present were Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr Bonita Douzart, Mary

Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Jerry Bowman Jr, Barry Rachal, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell.  Superintendent Keith Burton and Legal Counsel Reginald Abrams were also present.
