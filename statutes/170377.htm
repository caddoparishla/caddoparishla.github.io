TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 1. GENERAL SCHOOL LAW

PART IV-B. QUALITY SCIENCE AND MATHEMATICS PROGRAM
SUBPART D. QUALITY SCIENCE AND MATHEMATICS PROGRAM

 

17:377         Quality Science and Mathematics Program; oversight

  A. The activities of the council and the implementation of the program may be reviewed by the House Committee on Education and the Senate Committee on Education and the rules and regulations shall be subject to review as provided by the Administrative Procedure Act.

  B. The investment of the monies in the trust and equipping funds and the uses, administration, allocation, and management of the monies may be reviewed by and shall be subject to the oversight of the Education Funds Committee. Such review and oversight shall be done in the same manner as that provided in R.S. 17:3804 regarding the Education Funds Committee oversight authority over the State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education and the Board of Regents.

Added by Acts 1992, No. 1093, Sec. 1.
