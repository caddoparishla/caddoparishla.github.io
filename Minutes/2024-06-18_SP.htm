CPSB Special Session

Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon,Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell. Interim Superintendent, Leisa G. Woolfolk, and Legal Counsel Reginald Abrams was also present.
