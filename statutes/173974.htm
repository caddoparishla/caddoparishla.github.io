Title 17. Education

Chapter 42. Charter School Demonstration Programs Law

Part I. General Provisions

 

17:3974      Prohibitions; persons convicted of felony offenses

 

No person who has been convicted of or has pled nolo contendere to a crime listed in R.S. 15:587.1(C) shall be hired by a charter school as a teacher, substitute teacher, bus operator, substitute bus operator, or janitor, or as a temporary, part-time, or permanent school employee of any kind.
