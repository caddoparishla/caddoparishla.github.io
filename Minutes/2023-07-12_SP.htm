July 12, 2023

CPSB Special Session

 

The Caddo Parish School Board (CPSB) met in Special Session on July 12, 2023, at 4:30 p.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, Louisiana.  Board members present were Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, and Dottie Bell.  Also present were Superintendent Lamar Goree and Legal Counsel Reginald Abrams.  

 

President Vinson called the meeting to order at approximately 4:30 p.m., and Ms. Trammel gave the Invocation and led the Pledge of Allegiance.  

 

Visitors

None

 

1.05 Declare District Seat 7 Vacant and Call for Special Election. Ms. Trammel moved, seconded by Mrs. Bell, to approve the Proclamation declaring District 7 seat vacant as a result of the attached Judgment of the First Judicial District Court dated June 29, 2023, declaring the seat being held by Barbara Smith-Iverson vacant and that the Caddo Parish School Board call for a Special Election to fill said vacancy as provided in the Proclamation. The vote on the motion carried unanimously.

 

PROCLAMATION

WHEREAS, the Caddo Parish School Board has received a certified copy of the attached Judgment dated June 29, 2023 from the First Judicial District Court of Caddo Parish advising that the Caddo Parish School Board District 7 seat held by Barbara Smith-Iverson was declared vacant.  No appeal has been taken to the La. 2d Circuit Court of Appeal, and said Judgment is final.

WHEREAS, the above Judgment created a vacancy in Caddo Parish School Board District 7 which requires a temporary appointment and the unexpired term of office in which the said vacancy exists will end on December 31, 2026 and exceeds one year.

NOW THEREFORE the Caddo Parish School Board does hereby issue this Proclamation pursuant to LSA-R.S. 18:602(E)(2) ordering a special election to fill the said vacancy in Caddo Parish School Board District 7 for the remainder of the unexpired term as follows:

(1)           The Gubernatorial Primary Election shall be held on Saturday, October 14, 2023; and

(2)           The Gubernatorial General Election shall be held on Saturday, November 18, 2023.

The dates for qualifying for the candidates in the special election shall be August 8, 2023- August 10, 2023.

This Proclamation shall be published in the official journal of Caddo Parish and a copy thereof shall be sent by certified or registered mail to the Clerk of the First Judicial District Court of Caddo Parish, Louisiana and to the Louisiana Secretary of State as required by law.

 

1.06 Establish Procedures for Filling the District 7 Vacancy Mrs. Bell moved, seconded by Mr. Rachal, that the following procedures for filling the District 7 Position be advertised in the official journal.  The vote on the motion carried unanimously.

 

That applications for filling the Caddo Parish School Board District 7 position be addressed to the President of the CPSB and accepted by hand delivery at the Superintendent’s office located at 1961 Midway Avenue, Shreveport, La. until 4:00 p.m. on Wednesday, July 19, 2023;

That applications contain the following:

1)  The applicant’s date of birth;

2)  The applicant’s address and how long they have lived in District 7; and

3)    A short statement regarding why applicant desires to serve as interim board member from District 7.

That interviews of candidates will follow the following format:

FORMAT FOR INTERVIEW SESSION-(Up to 10 minutes per candidate)

I.                    Each candidate will present an opening statement addressing the following:

A.     His or her reasons for desiring to serve as interim board member from District 7;

B.     His or her special skills or experience to offer as a board member from District 7; and

C.     His or her perspective on the role of a board member

 

II.                 Each candidate will be allowed to make any final summary statement if time permits.

Interviews to be held at a special board meeting to be held on Thursday, July 20, 2023, at 3:30 p.m. at the Caddo Parish School Board office board room, 1961 Midway Street, Shreveport, LA;

Move that there be a special board meeting to be held on Thursday, July 20, 2023, at 3:30 p.m. for the purpose of interviewing candidates for the District 7 vacancy and for selecting an interim appointment.

 

1.07 Call a Special Board Meeting to Interview Candidates and Select an Interim Appointment for the District 7 Vacancy. Mr. Rachal moved, seconded by Mrs. McLain, to call a Special Board Meeting to Interview Candidates and Select an Interim Appointment for the District 7 Vacancy. The vote on the motion carried unanimously.

 

 Adjournment.  Ms. Green moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to adjourn.  The vote on the motion carried, and the meeting adjourned at approximately 4:45 p.m.
