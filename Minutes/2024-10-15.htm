October 15, 2024

CPSB Meeting

 

 

The Caddo Parish School Board (CPSB)  met on October 15, 2024 at 4:30 p.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, LA.  Board members present were Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel,

Christine Tharpe, Jerry Bowman, Jr., Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell.  Superintendent Keith Burton and Board Counsel Reginald Abrams were also present.

 

1.01 Invocation (Katie McLain, District 10)

 

1.02 Pledge of Allegiance (Southern Hills Elementary Students)

 

1.03 Roll Call President Yeates called the October 15, 2024 CPSB Meeting to Order

 

2.01 Consideration of Minutes

Move to approve the minutes of the August 27, 2024, September 3, 2024, September 11, 2024, September 17, 2024 and September 25, 2024 CPSB Meetings as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

Motion by Dottie Bell, second by Katie McLain.

Final Resolution: Motion Carries

Yes: Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, Dottie Bell

Abstain: Jerry Bowman, Jr.

 

3.01 Presentations and Recognitions

Dottie Bell introduced the owner of Gators and Friends in the audience. Barry Rachal welcomed the new Interim Board Member for District 7, Jerry Bowman, Jr. to the school board Mary Wood, Executive Director of Strategic Initiatives announced the following

presentations and recognitions: Musical Presentation, National Merit Semifinalists, LMEA District VIII Symphonic Honor Band Participants

 

3.02 Visitors

Tammy Bellamy, paraprofessional at BTW, asked why the pay is so low for paras and asked how they expect people to live on that salary because most paras have to work a 2nd job just to survive. Jackie Lansdale told the board that the Caddo school bus

drivers are paid $6K lower than drivers in other parishes. She welcomed Mr. Burton as our new Superintendent, and said that we need smaller class sizes. Jon Glover spoke about the minutes for board meetings that are placed on Board Docs do not have precise

details like the minutes did in 2014. Mary Nash Robinson welcomed Mr. Burton and new board member, Jerry Bowman, Jr.
