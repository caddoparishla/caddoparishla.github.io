May 28, 2024

CPSB Budget Work Session

 

 

The Caddo Parish School Board (CPSB) met on May 28, 2024, at 2:00 p.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, Louisiana.  Board members present were Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe,

Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, and Jessica Yeates.

The Interim Superintendent, Leisa G. Woolfolk, and Legal Counsel Reginald Abrams were also present.

1.01 Invocation (Dr. Bonita Douzart, District 5)

1.02 Pledge of Allegiance (Dr. Bonita Douzart, District 5)

 

1.03 Roll Call President Yeates called the meeting to order at approximately 2:00 p.m.

 

1.04 Visitors Ms. Jon Glover asked for a hard copy of the new budget.

 

1.05 Discussion 2024 -2025 CPSB Budget Discussion  Jeff Howard, Chief Financial Officer, gave an overview of the budget process and an overview of the proposed 2024-2025 CPSB Consolidated Annual Budget. Mr. Howard also gave a summary of the General

Fund. He stated an overview of the remaining funds would be presented at the next Budget Work Session scheduled for June 4, 2024.

 

1.06 Adjournment Move to adjourn the May 28, 2024 CPSB Budget Work Session.

Motion by Mary Trammel, second by Dr. Bonita Douzart

Final Resolution: Motion Carries

Yes: Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates
