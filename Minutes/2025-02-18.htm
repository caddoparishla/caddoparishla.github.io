3.01 Presentations and Recognitions Genevieve Nickelson, an 8th-grade student at Caddo Middle Magnet, gave an outstanding musical performance on the piano. Mary Nash-Wood, Executive Director of Strategic Initiatives, introduced Caddo’s

Student Leader for February. Isaiah Washington, a Green Oaks Performing Arts Academy senior, has demonstrated a deep commitment to his education and school community. Congratulations to Christine Hobart, the art teacher at Westwood

Elementary, for receiving the prestigious Louisiana Art Educator of the Year title. The Captain Shreve JROTC Rifle Team won 1st place in the Louisiana Civilian Marksmanship Program 3 position Army Air Rifle State Championship with an overall score of

1916. The shooters on the championship team were Cadet Fredrick Grozinger (1st place individual shooter award with 510 out of 600), Cadet Kristiana Logue, Cadet Marisol Willard, and Cadet Jolie Pratt. The 2nd place award in the JROTC Army State

Rifle Championship competition went to the Northwood High School, with an overall score of 1882. Caddo JROTC had three other teams in the top 10, with Caddo Magnet JROTC 4th place, CE Byrd JROTC 5th place, and North Caddo JROTC 6th Place.

Congratulations to all of the cadets
