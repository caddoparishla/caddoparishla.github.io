April 16, 2024

CPSB Meeting

 

 

Members present Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little,  Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell. Dr. T. Lamar Goree, Secretary, and Mr. Reggie Abrams, Board Counsel, were

also present.

 

President Jessica Yeates called the April 16, 2024, meeting to order. Board Member Don Little gave the Invocation, and students from Shreve Island Elementary led the Pledge of Allegiance.

 

CONSIDERATION OF THE MINUTES OF THE MARCH 5, 2024, and MARCH 19, 2024 CPSB MEETINGS. Ms. Trammel moved, seconded by Mr. Dixon, to approve the minutes of the March 5, 2024, and March 19, 2024, CPSB meetings as submitted in the

electronic mailout.  Vote on the motion carried unanimously.

 

PRESENTATIONS AND RECOGNITIONS.

Ms. Marquel Sennet, Director of Communications, introduced the Walnut Hill Choir as they sang “Happy Together” and “Disco Survives”.  The group is under the direction of Berenice Bertado. Then, Ms. Sennet announced Mrs. Amy Taylor, a second-grade

teacher at University Elementary, for being selected as a finalist for the Presidential Award in Excellence in Mathematics and Science Teacher. She added that this is the highest recognition that a K-12 grade science, technology, engineering, or mathematics

teacher may receive for outstanding teaching in the United States. Ms. Sennet stated that the Louisiana Department of Education announced Principal Robert Middleton of Caddo Magnet High as a semifinalist for the Louisiana Principal of the Year. Then, Ms.

Sennet congratulated Aashni Shah, a Junior at Caddo Magnet High, on receiving the Virginia K. Shehee Most Influential Young Woman Award.  She was selected out of six honorees who continuously show leadership, poise, and determination demonstrated

through meaningful acts of philanthropy for their fellow students and community.

 

VISITORS

The following visitors addressed the Board on matters of interest/concern: Jon Glover asked the Board about the budget and the schedule for the budget meetings; Carol Smith asked the Board to implement more programs at Green Oaks Performing Arts so that

students will return back to Green Oaks; Jordan Thomas shared progress in the Louisiana Legislature regarding education and support for certified teachers in the classroom. She also announced the “Most Improved Student” award ceremony on Thursday at

Captain Shreve High School.

 

ESTABLISH THE AGENDA AND PROPOSED CONSENT AGENDA

Superintendent Goree highlighted items for the Board’s consideration.  President Yeates announced the following items are on consent: 6.01-6.03, 7.01, 8.01, 8.03-8.07, and 13.02

 

Ms. Trammel moved, seconded by Mr. Rachal, to establish the agenda and consent agenda for the April 16. 2024, CPSB meeting as presented.  The vote on the motion carried unanimously.

 

CONFIRM THE CONSENT AGENDA

Mr. Rachal moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to confirm the consent agenda for the April 16, 2024, CPSB Meeting.  Vote on the motion carried, and the following is a summary of the Board’s action on the consent agenda items.

 

6.01 Personnel Recommendation The Board approved the Personnel Recommendation as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

6.02 Personnel Leaves  The Board approved the Personnel Leaves as recommended by the Superintendent and submitted in the electronic mailout

6.03 Personnel Resignations The Board approved the Personnel Resignations as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

7.01 Capital Project Bids The Board approved the bids as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout on the bid tabulation sheets: 1) Project 2025-239 "Huntington Restroom Renovation" be awarded to Daren Bailey, Inc. (Not OCC*),

Shreveport, LA, with a Base Bid, Alternate 1, Alternate 2 and Alternate 3, for the sum total of $496,928.00. The budget amount is $500,000.00. The project will be paid for with Capital Projects Funds. 2) Project 2025-241 "Youree Drive Restroom Renovation," be

awarded to Thrash Construction (Not OCC*), Shreveport, LA, with a Base Bid and Alternate 1, for the sum total of $389,000.00. The budget amount is $400,000.00. The project will be paid for with Capital Projects Funds. 3) Project 2025-214, "Byrd Baseball Field

Drive at LSUS," be awarded to M & M Builders (Not OCC*), Shreveport, LA, with a Base Bid, for the sum total of $312,400.00. The budget amount is $400,000.00. The project will be paid for with Capital Projects Funds and LSUS. 4) Project 2025-820, "Broadmoor

Health Clinic," be awarded to ELA Group (Not OCC*), Shreveport, LA, with a Base Bid and Alternate 1, for the sum total of $475,000.00. The budget amount is $400,000.00. The project will be paid for with ESSER Funds. 5) Project 2025-215, "Bethune/Oak Park

Bus Loop," be awarded to 3 Gen Construction (Not OCC*), Shreveport, LA, with a Base Bid and Alternate 1, for the sum total of $318,452.00. The budget amount is $300,000.00. The project will be paid for with Capital Projects Funds.

 

8.01 Property/Bus Rental Requests The Board approved Property/Bus Rental Request as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.03 Consideration to approve the renewal of the Lease Agreement between LA Tech University and CPSB for continued use of the Caddo Virtual Academy facility The Board approved the renewal of the Lease Agreement between LA Tech University and CPSB

for continued use of the Caddo Virtual Academy facility as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

8.04 Consideration to approve lease Agreement for Worley Observatory The Board approved the lease agreement for Worley Observatory Property between Caddo Parish School Board and Shreveport-Bossier Astronomical Society, Inc. as recommended by staff

and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.05 Consideration to approve revisions to Policy EBBC-Emergency Crisis Management, Policy EBCB-Video/Audio Monitoring of School Board Property, and remove Policy EBBI-Public Health Emergency. The Board approved revisions to Policy EBBC

Emergency Crisis Management, Policy EBCB-Video/Audio Monitoring of School Board Property, and remove Policy EBBI-Public Health Emergency as recommended by the Policy Review Committee and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.06 Consideration to approve the listing for sale of the Whitehall Property The Board approved the listing for sale of the Whitehall Property for the appraised amount of $5,250.00 as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.07 Consideration to approve the First Amendment to the Agreement to buy and sell Barret Paideia Academy  The Board approved the conditions in the First Amendment to the Agreement to buy and sell Barret Paideia Academy as recommended by staff and

submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

13.02 Request for Settlement Authority in Mariah Corbell vs Larry Culpepper and Caddo Parish School Board, Suit #640317-A, Date of Loss: 11/18/2021, Claim #7205257 The Board authorized the Request for Settlement Authority in Mariah Corbell vs Larry

Culpepper and Caddo Parish School Board, Suit #640317-A, Date of Loss: 11/18/2021, Claim #7205257 as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.02 Consideration to Approve Resolution and an Extract of Resolution to Request for Louisiana State Mineral Board Advertisement  Mr. Rachal moved, seconded by Mrs. Bell, to approve the proposed Resolution and Extract of Resolution to solicit

bids for oil and gas leases on Caddo Parish School Board Property as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout. After discussion, Ms. Green made a substitute motion, seconded by Dr. Douzart, to approve the proposed Resolution and

Extract of Resolution to solicit bids for oil and gas leases on Caddo Parish School Board Property located at Hillsdale Elementary and remove Northside Elementary Property from this agenda item. The vote on the motion failed, with Mr. Little, Ms. Trammel,

Mr. Dixon, Mrs. Tharpe, Mr. Rachal, Mrs.Yeates, and Mrs. Bell opposed. Then, the Board voted on the original motion to approve Resolution and an Extract of Resolution to solicit bids for oil and gas leases on Caddo Parish School Board Property as

recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.  The vote on the motion carried, with Ms. Green opposed, and Dr. Douzart not present at vote.

 

ADJOURNMENT. Ms. Green moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to adjourn.  The vote on the motion carried, and the April 16, 2024,

CPSB Meeting adjourned at approximately 5:25 p.m.
