February 20, 2024

CPSB Meeting

 

Members presentSteve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell. Dr. T. Lamar Goree, Secretary, and Mr. Reggie Abrams, Board Counsel, were also present.

 

President Jessica Yeates called the February 20, 2024, meeting to order. Board Member Jasmine Green gave the Invocation, and Pine Grove Elementary School students led the Pledge of Allegiance.

 

CONSIDERATION OF THE MINUTES OF THE JANUARY 8, 2024, JANUARY 9, 2024, AND JANUARY 23, 2024, CPSB MEETINGS. Mrs. Trammel moved, seconded by Mrs. Bell, to approve the minutes of the January 8, 2024, January 9, 2024, and January 23, 2024, CPSB meetings as submitted in the electronic mailout.  Vote on the motion carried unanimously.

 

PRESENTATIONS AND RECOGNITIONS. Marquel Sennet, Director of Communications, introduced 4th Grade Student at South Highlands, Palmer Pendley, as he performed on the piano the musical selection “Rain Drop Prelude” by Chopin.  Next, Ms. Sennet announced that two Caddo students were selected to join the nation’s most promising young scientists in the Regeneron Science Talent Search 2024 – Leslie Hunter of C. E. Byrd High School and Sophie Chen of Caddo Magnet High.  In this year's Regeneron Science Talent Search, Sophie advanced as a Top 40 finalist. Then, Southwood High School’s JROTC Unarmed Drill Team was recognized as the 1st Place Army, Louisiana State Drill Champions for 2024 making this outstanding drill team three-time consecutive Army Drill Champions for the State of Louisiana. Ms. Sennet introduced Caddo’s Outstanding Support Employee of the Year Finalist, Mr. Carlton Hardy.  Mr. Hardy is a Security Coordinator at Caddo Middle Career and Technology School. In conclusion, the 2023-2024 Teachers of the Year were recognized – Elementary: Rakeisha West, Riverside Elementary; Middle: ShaManda Williams, Fair Park Middle; High: Ryanne Comerford, C. E. Byrd High School.  These three outstanding teachers will now advance to the regional levels of competition.

 

VISITORS The following visitors addressed the Board: Christella Jackson supports the renaming of Northside Middle School; Jon Glover is concerned about school closures and consolidations; Karen Austin White supports the renaming of Northside Middle School; Martha Glover supports the renaming of Northside Middle School; Jacqueline Miller concerned with the crimes at the schools that were shown on the news and social media; Bernard Friday concerned with the lack of certified teachers in the schools.

 

ESTABLISH THE AGENDA AND PROPOSED CONSENT AGENDA Superintendent Goree highlighted items for the Board’s consideration.  President Yeates announced the following items are on consent: 6.03-6.04, 8.01-8.04, 8.06-8.07, and 13.01.

 

Ms. Trammel moved, seconded by Mrs. Bell, to establish the agenda and consent agenda for the February 20, 2024, CPSB meeting as presented. The vote on the motion carried unanimously.

 

CONFIRM THE CONSENT AGENDA Mr. Rachal moved, seconded by Mr. Dixon, to confirm the consent agenda for the February 20, 2024, CPSB Meeting.  The vote on the motion carried, and the following is a summary of the Board’s action on the consent agenda items.

 

6.03 Personnel Resignations. The Board approved the Personnel Resignations as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

6.04 Administrative Contract Renewals.  The Board approved the Administrative Contract Renewals as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.01 Property/Bus Rental Requests. The Board approved the Property/Bus rental requests as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.02 Consideration to approve the listing of the E. B. Williams Stoner Hill Elementary Property. The Board approved the property at 2127 C.E. Galloway Boulevard in Shreveport, Louisiana, to be listed for sale through our Listing Agent at U.L. Coleman, Inc. as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.03 Consideration to approve renaming Northside Middle School to Linear/Northside Middle School. The Board approved renaming Northside Middle School to Linear/Northside Middle School as recommended by Jasmine Green and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.04 Consideration to approve renaming Woodlawn Leadership Academy to Woodlawn High School. The Board approved the renaming of Woodlawn Leadership Academy to Woodlawn High School as recommended by Mary Trammel and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.05 Consideration to approve revisions of Policy BCBK (Executive Sessions), Policy DC (Annual Operating Budget), Policy DID (Audits), and Policy ED (Transportation Management). The Board approved revisions of Policy BCBK (Executive Sessions), Policy DC (Annual Operating Budget), Policy DID (Audits), and Policy ED (Transportation Management) recommended due to Legislative Updates and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.06 Consideration to Approve Resolution to authorize the Superintendent to sign the 2023 Shreveport Early Start Initiative Agreement. The Board Approved Resolution to authorize the Superintendent to sign the 2023 Shreveport Early Start Initiative Agreement on behalf of the Caddo Parish School Board as submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.07 Consideration to approve the Superintendent's Goals for the 2024 evaluation period. The Board approved the Superintendent’s Goals for the 2024 evaluation period as submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.11 Consideration to approve revision to Policy IKAD (Parent Conferences). The Board approved revision to Policy IKAD (Parent Conferences) as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.13 Consideration to approve the Purchase and Sale Agreement for the sale of the Northside Elementary Property (old site) located at 1950 Northside Road to Brown-Taylor Development for $125,000.00. The Board approved the Purchase and Sale Agreement for the sale of the Northside Elementary Property (old site) located at 1950 Northside Road to Brown-Taylor Development for $125,000.00 as recommended by staff and submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

13.01 Student Readmission Appeal Hearings. The Board approved staff recommendations for students AA, SB, AM, JH, RC, CB, TT, and CL, and parents/guardians are in agreement as submitted in the electronic mailout.

 

8.08 Consideration to direct the Administration of the Caddo Parish School system to develop and advertise an RFP for an Energy Performance Project. Board President Yeates announced this agenda item would be sent to the Long-Range Planning Committee.

 

8.09 Consideration to approve a Resolution of Congratulations and Commendations for Ms. Sharon Johnson. Dr. Terence Vinson asked that this item be removed from the agenda.

 

8.10 Consideration to approve a Resolution of Congratulations and Commendations for Mr. Robert Jackson. Dr. Terence Vinson asked that this item be removed from the agenda.

 

8.12 Consideration to authorize the Caddo Parish School Board President to sign a Letter of Support for the Historic Preservation of Barrett Paideia Academy. Mrs. Bell moved, seconded by Ms. Trammel, to authorize the Caddo Parish Board President to sign a Letter of Support for the Historic Preservation of Barrett Paidia Academy.  The vote on the motion carried. Dr. Douzart was not present at vote.

 

Adjournment. Mrs. Bell moved, seconded by Ms. Green, to adjourn the February 20, 2024 CPSB Meeting.  The vote on the motion carried, and the meeting was adjourned at 5:45 p.m.
