TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 4. LOUISIANA STATE UNIVERSITY AND AGRICULTURAL AND MECHANICAL COLLEGE

PART II. DIVISIONS, BRANCHES AND DEPARTMENTS

SUBPART C-1. LOUISIANA STATE UNIVERSITY HEALTH SCIENCES CENTER--SHREVEPORT

 

17:1516      Creation; administration

      A.  The Louisiana State University Health Sciences Center at Shreveport is hereby created and established at Shreveport, Louisiana.

     B. The Board of Supervisors of Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical College shall administer the affairs of the institution herein provided for.

Added by Acts 1965, No. 2, §§ 1, 2.  Amended by Acts 2005, No. 145, § 1, eff. July 1, 2005.
