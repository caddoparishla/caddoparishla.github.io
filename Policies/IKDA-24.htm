DISPLAY OF FLAG(S)

 

 

The Caddo Parish School Board believes that the flag of the United States is a symbol of unity, the values of our nation, the ideals embedded in our Constitution and represents the rights protected of all in our school district.

 

The School District shall purchase a United States and Louisiana flag, flagstaff and the necessary appliances for its display upon or near every utilized school and administrative building.  There shall be a United States of America flag on display in every assembly room of every utilized school.

 

The only flags that are permitted to be raised or displayed on or in any School District school, administrative buildings, and any/all other Caddo Parish School Board property shall be the United States flag and the flag of the State of Louisiana, in the traditional colors and arrangement in accordance with state and federal rules and regulations.

 

Consistent with national and state laws and regulations and this policy, the Superintendent of Schools shall develop rules and regulations for the proper custody, care, and display of the flag(s).

 

The flag, when it is in such condition that it is no longer a fitting emblem of display, should be retired through donation to an appropriate scouting, civic, or military organization or should be destroyed in a dignified way in accordance with United States Flag Code.

 

New policy:  June 19, 2024

 

 

Ref:    United States Flag Code Title 4, Chapter 1 Sec. 5
