August 27, 2024

CPSB Special Session

 

 

The Caddo Parish School Board (CPSB) met in Special Session on August 27, 2024, at 4:30 p.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, Louisiana.  Board members present were Steve Umling, Jasmine Green, Dr. Terence Vinson, Don Little, Dr.

Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Christine Tharpe, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell.  Interim Superintendent Leisa G. Woolfolk and Legal Counsel Reginald Abrams were also present.

1.02 Pledge of Allegiance (Christine Tharpe, District 8)

 

1.03 Roll Call President Yeates called the meeting to order at approximately 4:30 p.m.

 

1.04 Visitors Barbara Norton, Jon Glover, Sheila Wimberly, Jacqueline Miller, Rickey Hall, and Stephanie Williams

 

1.05 Update on Superintendent Search from McPherson & Jacobson, LLC.

Marquel Sennet, Director of Communications, introduced Dr. Barry Carol and Dr. Steve Joel from McPherson & Jacobson LLC.  McPherson & Jacobson have conducted superintendent searches all over the country and were hired to conduct an extensive

nationwide search for superintendent candidates. They announced there were 31 applicants, and two of those dropped out yesterday. In selection of the finalists, the firm announced they considered input from parents, staff members, students, and the

community. After they reviewed the results of almost 700 community surveys and 18 stakeholder meetings, McPherson & Jacobson narrowed the applicant pool down to six candidates: Keith Burton, Dr. Nia Campbell, Dr. Fateama Fulmore, Dr. Justin Hunter, Dr.

Cheryl Proctor, and Dr. Thomas Rogers. As each finalistâ€™s name was announced, a short video that the finalist submitted was shown to the board.

 

At the conclusion of the videos, each board member was given a score sheet to rank the finalists. Dr. Carol and Dr. Joel of McPherson & Jacobson totaled the scores submitted by the twelve school board members.  The total score for each finalist based on the

individual scores of each board member was as follows: Keith Burton (58), Dr. Cheryl Proctor (32), Dr. Thomas Rogers (26), Dr. Fateama Fulmore (21), Dr. Nia Campbell (20), Dr. Justin Hunter (4).

 

President Yeates called for a 15-minute recess at 6:10 p.m. and added an additional 10 minutes to it. The meeting resumed at 6:35 p.m.
