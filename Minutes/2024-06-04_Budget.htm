June 4, 2024

CPSB Budget Work Session

 

 

The Caddo Parish School Board (CPSB) met on June 4, 2024, at 2:00 p.m. in the board room at 1961 Midway, Shreveport, Louisiana.  Board members present were Jasmine Green, Don Little, Dr. Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Barry Rachal,

Katie McLain, Jessica Yeates, and Dottie Bell.  The Interim Superintendent, Leisa G. Woolfolk, and Legal Counsel Reginald Abrams were also present.

1.01 Invocation (Mary Trammel, District 6)

1.02 Pledge of Allegiance (Mary Trammel, District 6)

1.03 Roll Call President Yeates called the meeting to order at approximately 2:00 p.m.

1.04 Visitors Jon Glover had questions regarding the ESSR Funds.

 

1.05 Discussion 2024 - 2025 Budget Discussion Jeff Howard, Chief Financial Officer, gave an overview of the May 28, 2024 CPSB Budget Work Session.  He gave a summary of the Special Revenue Funds and Capital Project Funds. Mr. Howard stated the next

budget work session is scheduled for June 11 should the Board determine another meeting was necessary.  He added the Public Hearing and adoption is scheduled for June 18, 2024.

 

1.06 Adjournment Move to adjourn the June 4, 2024 CPSB Budget Work Session.

Motion by Jasmine Green, second by Mary Trammel

Final Resolution: Motion Carries

Yes: Jasmine Green, Don Little, Dr Bonita Douzart, Mary Trammel, Darrin Dixon, Barry Rachal, Katie McLain, Jessia Yeates, and Dottie Bell
