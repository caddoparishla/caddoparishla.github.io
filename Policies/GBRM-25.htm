TRAINING IN HUMAN TRAFFICKING AWARENESS

 

 

It is the policy of the Caddo Parish School Board that every employee with direct access to students be trained in human trafficking awareness by Free the Coalition to End Human Trafficking in Northwest Louisiana every two (2) years beginning the 2019 school year.  School Board members are authorized to participate in the training.

 

New policy:  April 16, 2019
